An R 'n' B starlet who has been romantically linked to rap music mogul Sean 'Diddy' Combs has had topless pictures of herself leaked on the internet.

Intimate photos of model turned R'n'B star Cassie Ventura, 22, hit the web yesterday and quickly spread to a number of showbiz blogs.

The singer whose debut single Me & U was a top ten hit in the UK is seen in the self-portraits taken on her mobile phone, looking seductive without her top on.

Cassie, 22, pointed the finger at computer hackers who she said breached her laptop to steal the photos.

The singer, who most recently made headlines after shaving her head, took to her page on social networking website Twitter.com to accuse someone of hacking into her computer and stealing the cheeky snaps.


She wrote:  'It seems that someone has hacked into my computer...that's real foul and evil... Now stop acting like you haven't seen a t**ty before.'

But her laidback response has caused some commentators to wonder whether the leak was internal. 

Cassie's career hasn't exactly been stellar since her 2006 hit, so it's unlikely that the saucy pictures which do it any harm.

In fact it might help to resurrect it, as she is at present described by ascerbic bloggers as Combs 'jump-off' - or bit on the side.

She also came under fire for poor live performances and since then Combs has been working with Cassie to develop her music career.

Cassie's second studio album Electro Love, is scheduled to be released in June this year.

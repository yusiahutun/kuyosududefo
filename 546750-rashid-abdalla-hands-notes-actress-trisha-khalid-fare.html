Rashid Abdalla Hands Out Notes to Actress Trisha Khalid: &quot;Fare&quot;

Publish date: 2024-09-09
  • Lulu Hassan celebrated her 40th birthday and members of the Jiffy Pictures production team attended the party
  • Among the attendees was curvy video vixen and actress Trisha Khalid, who appeared on the Becky show
  • After the colourful celebrations, Rashid Abdalla playfully gave Trisha money, saying she had no fare

Citizen TV anchor and film producer Rashid Abdalla has mesmerised social media users after gifting actress Trisha Khalid KSh 1,000.

Why did Rashid Abdalla give Trisha Khalid money?

In a hearty video shared via Instagram, Abdalla said Trisha lacked fare after she visited his home with other Becky cast.

The cast paid the couple a surprise visit on April 24, 2024, to celebrate Abdalla's wife, Lulu Hassan, as she turned 40.

They wheeled to the home a sumptuous four-layer black forest drip cake in honour of Lulu.

"Acting has its challenges. Today, I had a guest, Trisha, who had no fare to go back. When you see them out and about, they also have a life. You said your day's fare is KSh 200. Here is another for your lunch and supper. Work hard, Nairobi is tough," said Abdalla.

Wowed social media users said that, though made in jest, Abdalla's gesture toward Trisha was sweet.

How did Lulu Hassan celebrate 40th birthday?

Lulu shared a rare glimpse of their posh home during the fun-filled birthday celebration.

The house is spacious and features bubble lighting, according to online photos.

Its minimalist grey sofa, a statement flat-screen TV, royal curtains, a chic table and premium porcelain, spoke of luxurious elegance.

The media couple owns and runs Jiffy Pictures. The production company is behind some of Kenya's top soap operas, including Becky, Huba, Moyo, Aziza and Maria.

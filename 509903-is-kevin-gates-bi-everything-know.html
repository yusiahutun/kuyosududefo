Who is Kevin Gates?

Kevin Gates is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and entrepreneur. He was born on 5th February 1986 in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, USA. His father is African American, while his mother is Puerto Rican. Initially, his family had settled in New Orleans before moving to Baton Rouge.

He lost contact with his father at a young age but later reconnected during his teenage-hood. His father passed away when he was 14. At 17, he briefly joined Baton Rouge Community College and later earned a master’s degree in psychology through a prison program.

Career beginnings

Kevin began his career in 2007 by signing with Dead Game Records. He collaborated with local artists Webbie and Boosie Badazz, and they released their debut mixtape, Pick of Da Litter, in 2007. The following year, the trio released All or Nuthin. His music career was paused after he and Boosie were incarcerated in separate cases.

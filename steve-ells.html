Career

Steve Ells started his career as a sous chef at the Stars restaurant, where Jeremiah Tower trained him for two years. To open the Denver Chipotle taco shop, he took out a loan of about $85,000 from friends and relatives. The Denver University campus was not far from the Chipotle’s chosen location.

Tacos and burritos are what Chipotle Mexican Grill is famous for. They operate more than 1000 restaurants across 38 states, including Washington, D.C., Toronto, Ontario, and London, England. The court mandated that a 45-inch high wall be constructed at every restaurant close to California, where this Inc. employs close to 26,500 employees.

He was appointed CEO of the chain in 1993 and held the position until 2009, sharing the various duties with Monty Moran. He was selected CEO of the Year by ColoradoBiz magazine in 2007. Steve presently chairs the Land Institute’s board of directors, where he is in charge of all projects.

In 2010, Steve joined Curtis Stone, Lorena Garcia, and Bobby Flay on NBC’s judging and investment panel for America’s Next Great Restaurant. He made the decision to retire as CEO of Chipotle after so many years and began serving as executive chairman only in November 2017. Chipotle has started looking for a new CEO with necessary experience as a result of his decision. Ali Namvar and Robin Hickenlooper, who worked with Ells, are also on the Chipotle board.

He received discipline in 2017 for acting inappropriately while a staff member served with the wrong facing label. He yelled at them, and the whole thing was shared on Twitter, where it was made public.

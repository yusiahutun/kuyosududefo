Kristen Stewart Is the Face of a New Chanel Beauty Campaign

Publish date: 2024-06-06

It was only a matter of time before Kristen Stewart inked a big-time beauty deal, and it’s only natural that it be with Chanel. The actress has worked with the legendary fashion house on numerous occasions, fronting its handbag collection, eyewear range, and more—and she even played Coco Chanel herself in a short film directed by Karl Lagerfeld at the end of last year.

Now, K. Stew has been tapped as the face of Chanel’s latest makeup collection, called Collection Eyes 2016. The first photo from the campaign just broke—and you can bet that Kristen is sporting her signature smudgy smoky eye—with the entire series, photographed by Mario Testino, set to be released in March. Chanel said of the choice, “With her modern, magnetic beauty and free spirit, Stewart appears in a series of portraits embodying various facets of the contemporary Chanel woman.”

We’ve been coveting Kristen’s smoky-eye prowess since forever, so if this new collection will get us one step closer to perfecting the on-point look … well, you know exactly where our money is going come spring.

Ever Wondered What Celebrity Bedrooms Look Like? Find Out Here

Publish date: 2024-03-18

A celebrity’s bedroom will usually be luxurious, innovative, and very expensive. They usually have new technology, luxury fabrics, and bespoke furniture. However, if you shop carefully, then you might be able to pick up one or two things that celebrities have in their bedrooms for a reasonable price. You can also buy things second-hand, used, or on credit.

In this article, we will bring you a list of things that can usually be found in celebrity bedrooms. Armed with this information, you will be able to begin redecorating your bedroom.

Upholstered Beds

Upholstered bed frames are very popular among celebrities and society’s wealthiest. They’re comfortable, sturdy, and they look very cool. If you’re interested in finding out more specifics about upholstered beds, then you can check it here; make sure that you ask bed manufacturers questions about their upholstered beds.

Not all are the same, and some are better than others. You need to find beds that are crafted from the finest materials, especially if you want your own celebrity bedroom. Beds that are poorly made will fall apart quicker. They will also show signs of cosmetic wear much faster.

Statement Pieces

If you want to create your own celebrity bedroom, then you will need more statement pieces than just your bed. You’ll need to invest in a dresser, an armchair, and perhaps a few antique tables. You shouldn’t clutter your bedroom though. You need to find the right balance.

Antique statement pieces are usually best for older homes. If you have a newer house, then you should probably find newer furniture. With that said, you can mix and match old and new if you’re tasteful about it. Painted antique furniture can look very attractive when it’s added to a modern home.

Introduce a Rug

Every celebrity bedroom has a rug. If you live in an older home, then you should invest in an antique Persian, or a modern reproduction if you’re on a budget. If you don’t want a Persian and live in a newer home, then you should browse rug manufacturer websites and try to find the one that will match the rest of your furniture and your room’s theme.

There are many different rugs to choose from. In modern homes, plain rugs usually look much better than rugs decorated with patterns, especially if you’re going for a minimalistic look.  

Keep Floor Space Cleared

When you look at pictures of celebrity bedrooms, you seldom find them cluttered and messy. You need to make sure that you copy this and don’t clutter your bedroom. Your bedroom should look luxurious at all times, just like rich celebrities would.

A cluttered bedroom is far from luxurious. Keep magazines, books, clothes, and other household items off of your bedroom floor. Make sure that everything is in its place. If you have extension cords, then tuck them away and hide them. Even if you don’t have a lot of luxury furniture, the fact that your bedroom is orderly and clean will instantly stand out to guests.

Technology

If you want to have your own celebrity bedroom, then you need to introduce and add new technology to it. It’s very common to find televisions that recede into the floor or into cabinets in celebrity bedrooms. If this is too expensive for you, then you could just buy a television cabinet that you can open and close.

Celebrity bedrooms also tend to have blinds that are activated by remote. This might not be too expensive. You can pick up blinds with mechanisms like this for a reasonable price in quite a few places.

Hide Your Perfumes

In a celebrity bedroom, expensive perfumes and make-up is seldom displayed. Instead, it is tastefully stored in a cabinet or drawer. Don’t fall into the trap of feeling like you need to display all of your beauty products on your nightstand. Putting them away is much more tasteful, and makes your bedroom appear much more like a celebrity bedroom.

The same goes for moisturisers, balms, and creams. The only exception to this rule is if you have crystal perfume jars and bottles. These can look very tasteful when displayed on your nightstand. If they’re in their original packaging then put them away, however.

Add Plants

Adding plants to your bedroom can make it look very luxurious. It can also be very refreshing, particularly if you work in an office or don’t get outside much. A bedroom that has plants also tends to look a lot more expensive.

You can add small potted ferns to your nightstand and bed stand. You can also add hanging vines to your wall shelves and on top of cabinets and wardrobes. A bedroom with plants is also a much healthier place to sleep. Plants oxygenate the air and are therapeutic.

Everything Has a Place

In celebrity bedrooms, you will find that everything has a place. In a normal person’s bedroom, things are haphazardly scattered around. If you want to emulate a celebrity bedroom at home, then make sure that everything has somewhere to go. Don’t put things in places where they don’t belong. Your bedroom needs to look organised and meticulous.

When you begin to scatter things around, even if your bedroom is filled with luxury furniture and is tastefully designed, then it will look childish and messy. Clothes go in the wardrobe for example, not on the floor.

Artwork and Posters

Celebrity bedrooms always contain artwork and posters. Toulouse Lautrec prints are a great way to add some flair to your bedroom. If you don’t like that type of art, then you could browse photographs of celebrity bedrooms until you find some artwork that you like.

When you’ve found it, you can post a photograph of it on an art forum asking for an ID. When you get the artwork’s name you can buy a reproduction or a print of it. You can still hang it in a nice, gilt frame. You can also hang it in a much simpler, minimalistic frame.

If you’ve ever wondered what a celebrity bedroom looks like, then this is the only page that you need. Hopefully, you will have an answer to your question now. All of the attributes and qualities listed on this page are straight out of a celebrity bedroom.

MUST VISIT: TWO SISTERS BOUTIQUE

Stuart native Sarah Stracuzzi opens up her dream shop, Two Sisters Boutique, in the Stracuzzi Plaza in Stuart. The name, inspired by sharing a closet with her sister when she was a child, suits the shop perfectly. Entering the store, it’s as if you are walking into a shared closet with your sister or best friend. The atmosphere is inviting with racks well-stocked with the cutest affordable fashions, bags, jewelry and home accessories.

Two Sisters Boutique’s motto is “beautiful but reasonable.” It aims to provide reasonable prices that don’t break the bank.

Visit the shop at 2885 SE Ocean Blvd. in Stuart, or shop anytime at . Extended shop hours Friday and Saturday.

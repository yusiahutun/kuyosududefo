WWE diva Summer Rae reacts to leaked photo rumours after Paige SEX TAPE scandal

Publish date: 2024-08-25

After British-born Paige had a sex tape leaked online, rumours are rife that similarly compromising images are on the internet of other stars.

Former superstar Kaitlyn is said to have become the latest victim of internet hackers.

Pictures reportedly show intimate snaps of the brunette bombshell, 30, in a series of nude selfies.

And amid claims that more WWE stars could also be embroiled in the scandal, blonde diva Summer Rae has hit out online.

She posted: “As they say, don’t believe everything you see on the internet.

“There’s people out there with a lot of time on their hands and a big imagination.”

Kaitlyn – whose real name is Celeste Bonin – is yet to speak out about the rumours.

Mauricio Ochmann Bio

The dashing actor was born on November 16, 1977, in Washington D.C.U.S.A. His love for acting is said to have started at a young age, but it was not until he was a teenager that he got his first acting role in the television show La Otra Osa. Later he moved to Los Angeles to study acting at the Joanne Baron Studio, and after graduation, he got a supporting role in the American film Message in a Bottle, where he played the role of a mailman. His professional acting career began in 1998 when he starred as the lead actor Santiago Berriozabal in the Mexican film Azul Tequila, and the following year he was given another role in the film Háblame de Amor.

Over the years the actor could exert a certain influence not only in American or Mexican movies but also in Spanish movies. His films include Ladies’ Night 2003, A la mala, which was released in 2015, and Hazlo Como hombre in 2017. Among the long list of television series in which Mauricio Ochmann has appeared are series on the big screen such as Como en el cine, in which he played the leading role of Javier, and the series Miranda de Mujer el regreso, Amarte as Frijolito, Marina and Dame Chocolate. However, the actor is said to have stopped acting in the series Marina and Dame Chocolate due to some addiction problems he had to deal with. Mauricio Ochmann played in the series Rosa diamante between 2012 and 2013 and then moved to the series El Senor de Los Cielos, in which he played numerous roles that earned him a Telemundo award as a best bad boy in 2015. He played the male role in the film El Chema as José Maria, which was broadcast from 2016 to 2017. Mauricio Ochmann is also known for his appearance in the sitcom Easy, released in 2016, where he played the role of Martin.

Who Are His Parents?

The telenovela star had neither the privilege of being raised nor of meeting his biological parents, as he was adopted by a couple named Maria and Guillermo. However, the couple divorced over time, which led to Ochmann’s adoptive mother Maria moving with him to Mexico. Later she began a new relationship with a German man named Thomas Ochmann, whose surname the actor currently bears. He is said to have been raised by Maria together with her other children named Thomas, Christian, and Paulo.

Mauricio Ochmann Wife

Mauricio Ochmann is the husband of the Mexican model and actress Aislinn Derbez, with whom he played the role of Santiago in the film A La Mala. The couple sealed the marriage on May 28, 2016, at a wedding ceremony that took place in Mexico. They have a lovely daughter named Kailani, a unique Hawaiian name that the couple chose as a reminder of the amazing trip to Hawaii that led to the birth of their lovely daughter, as Aislinn revealed on her Instagram page.

Before his current marriage, the talented actor was married to Maria Jose del Valle Prieto in 2008, and the couple has a daughter named Lorenza Ochmann. The two separated privately, as no details or reasons for their divorce were revealed.

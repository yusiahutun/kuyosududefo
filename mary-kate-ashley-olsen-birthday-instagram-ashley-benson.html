Olsen Twins Give a Super-Rare Birthday Shoutout to Ashley Benson

Publish date: 0001-01-01
  • Mary-Kate and Ashley Olsen made a very rare social media appearance to wish actress Ashley Benson a happy 30th birthday.
  • Benson is reportedly a superfan of the twin fashion moguls' two lines, Elizabeth and James, and The Row.

Public appearances from Mary-Kate and Ashley Olsen are essentially a total rarity, so you can imagine the excitement actress Ashley Benson felt when she received a custom shout-out from the twin fashion moguls.

Benson, who turned 30 today, shared the supersweet message on her Instagram, where both of the twins wished Benson in unison a “magical” birthday. The Pretty Little Liars alum is reportedly a dedicated fan of the Olsen’s two clothing labels, Elizabeth and James, and The Row.

“Happy birthday, Ashley,” the Olsen sisters say in the clip. “I hope this year is one of the best ones yet. Sending you lots of love.” The actresses-turned-designers are clad in their signature style during the quick message, both donning black wool jackets and tinted sunglasses, with Ashley letting her wavy tresses run free and Mary-Kate rocking a slicked-back, middle-parted bun.

Mary-Kate even goes on to grant Benson an official invitation to potentially meet and become friends, ending the clip by saying, “So magical and can’t wait to meet you soon,” while Ashley blows a kiss to the camera. Legends only. Benson captioned her appreciation for the iconic clip simply, “Dreams do come true.”

The surprise shout-out from Mary-Kate and Ashley wasn’t the only birthday love Benson received. Her girlfriend, Cara Delevingne, made sure to show some Instagram appreciation as well, with an awww-worthy photo gallery of some of their sweetest moments.

“I feel like I’ve known you my entire life and I am so proud of watching you grow into the woman that you always dreamt of being,” Delevingne posted. “I love you beyond words my sprinkles, choonchi, angel face, grumpy ass, sweet cheeks, never boring, buttmunch, Benson.”

Dual Instagram shout-outs from the love of your life and your favorite style icons? That’s a happy birthday, indeed.

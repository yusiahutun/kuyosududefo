Conor McGregor's controversial suit from Floyd Mayweather bout turned into Covid-19 mask

Publish date: 2024-02-07

One of Conor McGregor ’s controversial suits has now been made into a face mask by the Irishman’s tailor.

McGregor famously wore a suit brandishing “F*** you” ahead of his fight with Floyd Mayweather in 2017

The clothing went down a storm with tailor David August Heil making the pinstripe suit available to buy for a £5,000.

But now Heil has designed a rang of cloth face maskes and is using the iconic suit for some PPE.

They don’t come cheap with each mask costing £157 though Heil’s website claims that he will give five medical masks to "hard-hit hospitals and clinics" for every cloth mask he sells.

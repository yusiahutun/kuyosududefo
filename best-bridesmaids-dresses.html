Ballet Belles

14

Festival Redux

ImaxTree

Don't confuse this trend with classic boho–these textured, hand-embroidered and gossamer looks have a bit more polish. Lookout for tie-dye prints, flowing maxi skirts, frayed hems and lightweight silks–this printed trend packed with pops of color is perfect for beach and tropical weddings alike. Share your go-to palette (like the above cool pastels and mustard) with your 'maids so that they don't get lost in the array of technicolor options out there. Pictured above: Zimmermann, Chloé and Roberto Cavalli Fall 2016.

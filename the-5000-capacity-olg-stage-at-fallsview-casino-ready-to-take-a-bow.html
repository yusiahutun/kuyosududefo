The 5,000-Capacity OLG Stage at Fallsview Casino Ready To Take A Bow

Publish date: 2024-04-18

NIAGRA FALLS (CelebrityAccess) — Mohegan Entertainment welcomed their latest venue, the OLG Stage at Fallsview Casino in Niagra Falls, Ontario.

With a capacity of 5,000, the theater features state-of-the-art sound and lights and a seating configuration that ensures fans are no farther than 150 feet from the stage.

The OLG Stage at Fallsview Casino is located on the Fallsview Casino Resort property in the heart of one of Canada’s busiest tourist destinations, regularly attracting more than 13 million people annually to the region.

The theater was initially planned to open in 2020 but has not hosted a show due to occupancy restrictions from the pandemic and other technical challenges. However, an official grand opening event for the OLG Stage is expected to be announced soon.

When it opens later this month, the theater will join Mohegan Entertainment’s existing portfolio of venues, including Mohegan Sun Arena in Uncasville, CT, and the under-construction 15,000-seat Inspire Arena in South Korea.

Shows that have been announced for the OLG Stage At Fallsview Casino include Canada’s Got Talent (10/19-10/23), +Live+ (10/29), The I Love The 90’s Tour (11/3), Joey Yung (November 20) and Michael Bolton (December 16).

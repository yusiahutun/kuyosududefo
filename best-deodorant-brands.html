The 16 Best Deodorant Brands for 2024

Publish date: 2024-04-23

Best With Skincare Ingredients

Dove

Best With Skincare Ingredients

Dove

Pros
  • Lovely, light fragrances

Deodorants can often have irritating ingredients, but Dove puts skincare first. Take the new VitaminCare+ Aluminum Free Deodorant Stick, which has vitamin B3 to even skin tone as well as help keep odor at bay for up to 72 hours.

Customer Review:

"I’ve been dealing with lots of hormonal issues/changing which was causing me to have some nasty b.o. I’ve always used men’s deodorant, chock full of aluminum. Not only does this keep me from sweating and smelling horrible and also smells absolutely amazing. Two thumbs up from this gal." —Rachael

Best for Hyperhydrosis

Certain Dri

Best for Hyperhydrosis

Certain Dri

Pros
  • Works for excessive sweat
Cons
  • May be irritating due to high aluminum concentration

"For those who complain of excessive sweating, I recommend Certain Dri or Dove Clinical protection—these have higher antiperspirant percentages than a typical antiperspirant stick," says Castilla, who notes that "because of the higher antiperspirant percentages, these last two products are generally more irritating and less likely to work for sensitive skin."

Customer Review:

"Usually this type of product falls short but this works GREAT. I did not expect this to work so well. Considering buying in bulk so I don’t run out. Wonderful product."—ams

Derm Pick

Vanicream

Derm Pick

Vanicream

Pros
  • Suitable for sensitive skin

"The most common reason I recommend a certain deodorant brand is for people with sensitivities or rashes in their armpits," says Castilla, who often recommends Vanicream, "because it is free of dyes, fragrances, masking fragrances, and many irritating ingredients while still providing effective odor control."

Customer Review:

"My underarms began to break out and/or blister with strong deodorants, but the aluminum-free formula didn't feel like it stopped any odor, so I figured I'd give Vanicream a try. This stuff is actually amazing! It is super gentle on skin without compromising odor defense. Only one thing to note: it is not an antiperspirant. You will still sweat, but you will do so without smelling." —Cosette

Best pH-Balancing Deodorant

Megababe

Best pH-Balancing Deodorant

Megababe

Pros
  • Supports your microbiome

All of Megababe's aluminum-free deos, like the Smoothie Deo, smell fresh and fun, glide on easily, and feature odor-busting ingredients like fruit enzymes and postbiotics. If you do have to reapply, you'll be happy to pull this cute tube out of your bag.

Customer Review:

"I have tried so many aluminum-free deodorants over the years and this is the first one that controls odor for the entire day. I just stocked up, because I never want to be without!" —Amanda

Best Range of Products

Secret

Best Range of Products

Secret

Pros
  • Affordable price point

There's a good chance Secret was your first antiperspirant, and with trustworthy formulas like the Original Fresh Powder Deodorant, which is pH-balanced and has long-lasting odor and sweat protection, the brand maintains its GOAT status.

Customer Review:

"I've been using secret since teenage years!! I keep in my gym bag, one in bathroom, in my travel bag. Gentle on my skin with no pore clogging, superb effectiveness and not overwhelming perfumed."

Best for Clinical Protection

  • Lasts up to 72 hours

In addition to having the maximum sweat protection allowed, Degree Clinical Protection Shower Clean Antiperspirant & Deodorant Stick features the brand's MotionSense® Technology, which is activated when you start moving.

Customer Review:

Best Antiperspirant for Sensitive Skin

Carpe

Best Antiperspirant for Sensitive Skin

Carpe

Pros
  • Nourishing cream formula

For patients with sensitive skin who want to use an antiperspirant, Castilla often recommends Carpe and Dove, which she says contain "effective antiperspirants and are well formulated and generally non-irritating."

Customer Review:

"I have struggled with sweating my entire life. I have finally found a deodorant that works! I’ve been using this product for about 2 years and while it’s more expensive than any other deodorant I’ve used, I will never go back to anything else!"—Joellen

Best for Full Body

Lume

Best for Full Body

Lume

Pros
  • Can be used on underarms, feet, privates, and more
Cons
  • Doesn't have strong sweat protection

Been thinking about trying Lume's whole body deo? Castilla notes that they are formulated with mandelic acid, which "can help reset the microbiome and control odors."

Customer Review:

"This stuff is actual magic. No other deodorant has ever kept the smell away. Ever. And I’ve tried a bunch throughout the years. The only downside is it doesn’t keep me from sweating. I would much rather have wet armpits than stinky ones though." —Adrian

Best Refillable Deodorant

Saltair

Best Refillable Deodorant

Saltair

Pros
  • Made with BHA and zinc ricinoleate to neutralize odors

Deodorant is probably one of the products we have to buy (and re-buy) most, so refillable packaging seems like a no-brainer. You'll want to try all the scents from Iskra Lawrence's body care brand Saltair, so it's lucky the refills can be mixed and matched.

Customer Review:

"This is my new favorite deodorant. I feel my skin has lightened and softened since using this and the scent lasts for hours. No ingrown hair bumps under my arms and the absorption is good as well. I will never go back to regular deodorants." —Shann

Best Roll-On

Ban

Best Roll-On

Ban

Now 14% Off

Pros
  • Completely clear

Ban is known for their ultra-clear roll-on antiperspirant formulas that won't leave those annoying white marks on clothes. There are several scents to choose from, but the brand's unscented variety is a fave for those who are sensitive to fragrance.

Customer Review:

"I’ve used this deodorant for about 15+ years. It works! Before Ban, I tried different brands, stick and roll on and I would smell after a couple of hours after showering and applying. I also have 1 stubborn underarm that would smell so bad. Ban has been the only brand that works for me, and I love it has an unscented option." —gpolaris

Best Luxe Scents

Salt & Stone

Pros
  • Has skincare ingredients like hyaluronic acid

Santal & Vetiver, Neroli & Basil, Black Rose & Oud—no, these aren't perfumes, they're Salt & Stone deos. Not only do their products smell genuinely fantastic, but the brand has nailed the "so fancy you'll actually want to leave it out" vibe.

Customer Review:

"This stuff is my HOLY GRAIL!!! I have been trying a ton of aluminum free deodorants and have been struggling to find one that 1. Smells good and 2. Actually masks and holds sweat. This does the wonders of both. Highly recommend" —giannaf5

Best for Workouts

Best for Workouts

Duradry

Pros
  • Creamy formula won't tug or pull at skin

You don't have to be hitting the gym to appreciate Duradry's AM Deodorant & Antiperspirant, but it's a great buy if you happen to be. It comes in four scents to suit your mood, has perky packaging, and really, truly works against a major sweat sesh.

Customer Review:

"This deodorant works wonders and I now buy it for my pre-teen. She was having issues finding a strong deodorant, she's in gymnastics and is very active so nothing in the stores seemed to work. Duradry does and we both love it!" —Veronica O.

Michelle Obama Seemingly Upgraded Her Engagement Ring Get The Details On Her Stunning New Rock!

Publish date: 2024-07-07

What a lucky lady! Michelle Obama has been super busy lately promoting her new memoir, Becoming, and eagle-eyed fans have noticed that Barack Obama‘s wife seems to be sporting a recently upgraded engagement ring and wedding band in new photos.

In an exclusive new interview, Closer Weekly chatted with Kathryn Money, Vice President of Strategy and Merchandising at Brilliant Earth, about how the former First Lady’s original ring compares to her updated bling.

“Michelle’s original engagement ring features a 1-carat round diamond in a classic yellow gold solitaire setting. Depending on the quality and specific characteristics of the center diamond, we estimate the cost of the ring at $5,000 to $20,000,” Kathryn told Closer of the engagement ring Barack gave Michelle prior to their October 1992 wedding. To get the look of Michelle’s original engagement ring, check out Brilliant Earth’s lookalike rings, the 2mm Comfort Fit, Elodie, and Petite Comfort Fit.

As for her new rock, Kathryn told us, “Michelle’s upgraded engagement ring also has a timeless look befitting her overall style, with some additional sparkle. Her new elegant ring features an approximately 3-carat princess or cushion cut center diamond in a white gold pavé diamond accented setting.”

“Her new wedding ring matches perfectly for a seamless bridal set. Depending on the quality and specific characteristics of the center diamond, we estimate the cost of the ring at $30,000 to $50,000,” Kathryn added. To get the look of Michelle’s upgraded engagement ring, consider these lookalike Brilliant Earth styles, the Valencia, Petite Shared Prong, and Sienna.

While recently promoting her new book, Michelle gushed that she and Barack are now “finding each other again” after 26 years of marriage and leaving the White House behind. “We have dinners alone and chunks of time where it’s just us — what we were when we started this thing: no kids, no publicity, no nothing. Just us and our dreams,” she revealed. They seem like such a cute couple!

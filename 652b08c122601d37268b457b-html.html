Hollywood couple Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds donate $1m to Red Cross for Gaza relief

Publish date: 2024-06-25

Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds have once again shown their generosity by donating $1 million to the International Committee of the Red Cross (ICRC) to support children in Israel and Gaza. This power couple, who are known for their philanthropy, have always been passionate about contributing to causes that are close to their hearts.

According to a The Hollywood Reporter's source, Lively and Reynolds firmly believe in providing financial aid to organizations that help those in need. Their previous donations include matching up to $1 million for the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees in response to the Russia-Ukraine conflict.

They also donated $500,000 to Water First Education & Training Inc., a charity that focuses on providing safe water to Indigenous communities. Additionally, they contributed $1 million to Feeding America and Food Banks Canada during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Their latest donation to the ICRC comes at a critical time. The ongoing Israel-Hamas conflict has resulted in numerous casualties and widespread devastation. Lively and Reynolds' contribution will support the ICRC's efforts to provide medical supplies, treat the injured, and assist those who are being held hostage.

The ICRC has been actively involved in providing assistance to Gaza. On the day of the Hamas terrorist attacks, they sent a truck filled with medical supplies, including body bags and stretchers, to a hospital in Gaza. The organization is also working tirelessly to assess the damage to sewage and water treatments, as well as establish contact with the hostages' families.

They hope their example will inspire others

Lively and Reynolds' donation is particularly significant as they are the first celebrities to publicly provide financial aid specifically for Israel and Gaza during this ongoing conflict. Their support serves as an inspiration for others to step forward and contribute towards alleviating the suffering caused by the war.

While the situation in Israel and Gaza remains tense, with Israel's military preparing for further action against Hamas, celebrities have been expressing their solidarity with the innocent victims of the conflict. Jennifer Garner, Adam Sandler, Yara Shahidi, Kerry Washington, and Martha Stewart are among those who have spoken out in support.

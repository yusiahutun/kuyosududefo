Lenny Bruce was born in Long Island, NY on October 13, 1925. Legendary stand up comedian and social critic who became a counterculture icon after he was repeatedly arrested for obscenity. He briefly adopted the stage name, Lenny Marsalle, while working at his mother’s stage shows.

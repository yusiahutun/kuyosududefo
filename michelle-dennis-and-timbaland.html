About

Timbaland is a 51 year old American Music Producer. Born Timothy Zachery Mosley on 10th March, 1972 in Norfolk, Virginia, USA, he is famous for producing and writing many hit songs in collaboration with various popular artists including Aaliyah, Missy Elliott, Justin Timberlake, Jay-Z, Nas, Ludacris, Bubba Sparxxx, Madonna, Rihanna, OneRepublic, Brandy, Drake, Rick Ross, and many more, in addition to contributing as a rapper and releasing several albums and songs, as well as being the founder of the record label Mosley Music Group in a career that spans 1992–present and 1986–present. His zodiac sign is Pisces.

Zendaya Just Wore a Side Boob-Baring Spider Web Dress

Publish date: 2024-02-03

Zendaya seriously understood the assignment with her latest look that screamed Spider-Man, but make it sexy. On Monday, the actress attended the premiere of her latest flick, Spider-Man: No way Home at the Regency Village and Bruin Theatres in Los Angeles, Calif., in a sultry and on-theme gown that has us wrapped up in her web.

For the occasion, Zendaya wore a plunging, nude Valentino dress embellished with intricate black beaded spider web designs. The garment scooped low along the sides and the back, and nearly cut up to her hip in a massive slit that showed off her legs for days. She accessorized with black pointy-toe pumps, diamond drop earrings, and a lace-and-mesh eye mask.

The actress opted for black and blue geometric eyeliner that created an exaggerated cat eye — further playing into the Catwoman aesthetic. She paired the statement eye with a nude lip and butt-length cornrow braids.

Zendaya, who plays leading lady MJ, was joined on the carpet by her co-star and boyfriend Tom Holland, who wore a brown satin suit with a double-breasted jacket and black tie. According to Entertainment Tonight, the actor stopped in his tracks once Zendaya stepped onto the carpet.

"I think Zendaya just showed up," Holland said to the publication when the crowd went wild. "That sounds like Zendaya just showed up." He then paused from his interview to watch his girlfriend in all her glory. The two later posed together smiling and laughing together. We can't wait to see this movie, because these two already have our Spidey senses tingling.

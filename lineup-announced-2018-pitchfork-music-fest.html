Lineup Announced For 2018 Pitchfork Music Fest

Publish date: 2024-04-24

CHICAGO (CelebrityAccess) — Influential Chicago-based music blog Pitchfork announced the full lineup for the 2018 return of their popular music festival.

This year’s Pitchfork Music Festival will take place in Chicago’s Union Park from July 20 to July 22, with a list of headliners that include Tame Impala, Fleet Foxes, and The War On Drugs.

Other new additions to the Pitchfork 2018 lineup include Blood Orange, Japanese Breakfast, Nilüfer Yanya, and Circuit des Yeux.

As well, on Sunday, Ms. Lauryn Hill closes the festival with a 20th-anniversary performance of her seminal album The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill.

Tickets to Pitchfork Music Festival are on sale for $75 a day or $175 for a three-day pass. The Pitchfork +PLUS upgrade is $375 for a three-day pass and includes a variety of exclusive amenities such as premium food vendors, private specialty bars, and premium air-conditioned bathrooms.

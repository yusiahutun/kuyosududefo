What happened to Bridget Fonda? Hollywood royalty resurfaces, explains why she left Hollywood

Publish date: 2024-08-18

The 'Single White Female' star and niece to Jane Fonda hasn't made a movie since 2002

    Remember Bridget Fonda? Now 59, the former actress — who hails from a bonafide Hollywood dynasty — was a busy movie star in the 1980s and 1990s, but hasn't acted in over two decades, and sightings of have been extremely scarce. But the star, now a married mom, briefly resurfaced at LAX earlier this week, offering a Daily Mail photographer a succinct explanation for her retirement, which is decidedly permanent.

    When asked if she'd ever make movies again, even with any director of her choice, Bridget answered repeatedly "no," and then explained "I don't think so, it's too nice being a civilian."

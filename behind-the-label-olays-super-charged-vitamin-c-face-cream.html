OLAY's Super-Charged Vitamin C Face Cream

Publish date: 2024-09-14

When you think of moisturizer, you might think of OLAY Regenerist—makes sense, seeing as the brand's signature red jars are synonymous with good skincare. (Or purple jars, for fans of the retinol line.) In fact, 20 jars of the iconic OLAY Regenerist Micro-Sculpting Cream are sold per minute worldwide. Now there's a new formulation added to the Regenerist rainbow. It comes housed in a sunny orange jar that hints at the juicy goodness inside. Welcome, OLAY Regenerist Vitamin C + Peptide 24 Facial Moisturizer.

The cream is part of the brand's new Vitamin C + Peptide 24 collection, which also includes a serum, facial cleanser and eye cream with brightening benefits. The moisturizing formula builds on time-tested OLAY science to bring together powerful active ingredients that can address multiple skincare goals at once. "The OLAY Vitamin C + Peptide not only provides two times brighter skin but also boosts skin cell turnover and corrects the look of uneven tone and texture. The peptide in the formula, combined with niacinamide, is added to help transform skin texture" claims Dr. Rolanda Wilkerson, Senior Beauty Scientist at Proctor and Gamble. Powerhouse ingredients like vitamin C, vitamin B3 and lactic acid work together to improve the look of uneven skin tone, deliver a burst of hydration, and impart a lit-from-within glow in a way that few formulas can pull off.

Here, we take a look behind the label to see what goes into creating one of the most hardworking face creams on the shelf—and why it belongs on yours, too.

What's inside the jar

The all-star in this formula is vitamin C. This powerful antioxidant is known for its ability to protect skin from free radicals and brighten skin tone. "We use a specific form of vitamin C that is very stable. What makes this product unique is the combination of ingredients in addition to the stable form of vitamin C, which work together to brighten skin, provide an even skin tone and improved texture," says Dr. Wilkerson. To maximize the benefits of this less reactive vitamin c that can easily combine with other ingredients, OLAY scientists have paired it with vitamin B3. Also known as niacinamide, this super-gentle ingredient has the ability to hydrate to help with surface skin cell turnover and is a potent skin-brightening ingredient in its own right. Vitamin C and niacinamide can work via two different mechanisms to interfere with the pigment appearance process, which can help improve the look of dull skin and discoloration.

In this vibrant jar, you'll also find lactic acid, a type of alpha-hydroxy acid and mild exfoliant that can help remove the dead skin cells that could otherwise pile up and impart a dull appearance. Lactic acid also minimizes the appearance of wrinkles and loss of firmness.

But of course, the OLAY Regenerist Vitamin C + Peptide 24 Facial Moisturizer doesn't stop there; it's packed with peptides, too. For the uninitiated, a peptide is a naturally occurring molecule found throughout the body. It can help out with everything from digesting food to building muscle; in the skin, it works to boost resilience. This formula contains a peptide-fatty acid combination (palmitoyl being the fatty acid, which allows it to penetrate through the skin's lipid barrier). It works in the same way as the skin's own peptides to support the natural renewal process in order to improve its overall appearance. Studies have shown that it reduces the look of fine lines and wrinkles, delivering smoother, firmer skin.

The peptide in the formula, combined with niacinamide, is added to help transform skin texture.

But, just as important as what's inside is knowing what's not inside. The dermatologist-tested cream is formulated without parabens, phthalates, synthetic dyes, or mineral oil. So you can slather it on, knowing that you're nourishing your skin without potentially harmful ingredients.

What it does for skin

Like many of OLAY's moisturizers, OLAY Regenerist Vitamin C + Peptide 24 Facial Moisturizer offers 24-hour hydration to keep skin healthy and functioning properly. So while it does offer plenty of bonus benefits, it serves as a powerful moisturizer in its own right.

In addition, the three star ingredients in this velvety formula work better together to help improve the look of uneven skin tone to bring out your inner glow. It visibly brightens dull skin and corrects the look of uneven tone and texture, acting as a wake-up call for your face. Best of all, you get all of these benefits in the form of a lightweight cream with a silky texture that melts effortlessly into skin. It also features a subtle citrusy scent, making the experience that much more refreshing in the mornings—and serving as a natural counterpart to the eye-catching orange shade of the jar.

How to max out its benefits

If you're looking to get your glow on, the OLAY Regenerist Vitamin C + Peptide 24 Facial Moisturizer is worth adding to your regular skincare routine. It's designed to be applied morning and night for the best results and should always be paired with an SPF during the daytime. (We're fans of OLAY's Regenerist Hydrating Mineral Sunscreen SPF 30, which offers sheer protection for skin via mineral-based active ingredients.) An advantage of pairing the two is that the combination of vitamin C and sunscreen have a synergistic effect, giving your skin the max amount of sun protection when you're outside.

Bottom line? OLAY Regenerist Vitamin C + Peptide 24 Facial Moisturizer is one of those overachieving skincare formulas that can take the place of a daily vitamin C serum and face cream, allowing you to get the most out of your skincare routine—and keep skin healthy 24/7.

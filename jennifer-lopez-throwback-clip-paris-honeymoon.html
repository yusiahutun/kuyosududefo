Jennifer Lopez Shares a Throwback Clip From Her Paris Honeymoon with Ben Affleck

Publish date: 2024-02-13

Jennifer Lopez is looking back at her luxurious summer travels.

The multi-hyphenate marked Throwback Thursday this week with a clip from her Paris honeymoon. In the video, shared on her JLo Beauty Instagram account, the star lounges in a full-marble bathroom in France, while filming herself on a phone decorated with a black monogrammed "JLo" case. Her accessories match the opulent setting, as she wears large diamond earrings, a matching bracelet, and several rings, with her honey-blonde hair piled atop her head.

After filming a mirror selfie, the newlywed pans across the room, showing a peek of the Eiffel Tower outside of a window, to zoom in on a white robe that reads, "Just Married" in gold script.

"Self care in Paris is always a good idea," she wrote.

The Maid in Manhattan actress and her husband Ben Affleck enjoyed a PDA-filled Paris vacation with their blended family last July, following their surprise Vegas nuptials. Mr. and Mrs. Affleck later held a second lavish ceremony at the Good Will Hunting actor's Riceboro, Georgia, estate in August, followed by a weeklong honeymoon in Italy.

The couple have been relatively low-key since arriving back in Los Angeles post-honeymoon. In early September, the couple were spotted continuing their honeymoon vibes in coordinating all-white outfits, during a family trip to the Malibu Chili Cook-Off.

BG020/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

In a recent interview with BAZAAR.com, the "Waiting for Tonight" singer shared the five fashion pieces that she cannot live without: "a pair of black Dolce&Gabbana sunglasses, of course, a great pair of well-fitting jeans, a blazer or coat, an iconic bag that can go with every outfit, and a statement piece of jewelry."

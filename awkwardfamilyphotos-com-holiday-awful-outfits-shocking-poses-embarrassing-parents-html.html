U.S. creators Mike Bender and Doug Chernack get millions of hits every month to their website, which allows readers to share those family photos that should really be hidden away.

Mexican hat: Some children just love to be different, but his parents don't look particularly impressed

Ouch: This looks like one of the most painful positions a man could ever be in, but you bet it was the dad's idea

The childhood friends got the idea for the website after Mr Bender saw an awkward holiday photo hung up in his parents' house - and thought many others must have the same.

They have also launched the Embassy Suites Awkward Family Vacation Photo Contest to find the most awkward photo submitted by users in the U.S. and Canada.

The website says it especially welcomes photos with sunburn, matching outfits and pyramid poses on the beach - and entries are being accepted for another two weeks.

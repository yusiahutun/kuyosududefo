Katie Holmes' head-to-toe leather look

Publish date: 2024-08-20

Katie Holmes has mastered one of the trickiest looks of all: head-to-toe leather.

The actress, who was seen out in New York City this week, wore a leather look consisting of a black shirt and matching trousers. While top-to-toe leather might sound daunting, or conjure up images of the Beckhams in their Noughties matching outfits, it's actually very wearable if done right.

Here's why we loved her look and how to make it work in your own wardrobe:

Gotham//Getty Images

The look

Holmes' chic ensemble – perfect for the autumn/winter season – consisted of a leather shirt, matching wide-leg leather trousers, a black handbag and silver metallic pointed heels. Beauty-wise, she kept things relaxed and minimal with glowy make-up and her hair in beach-worthy waves, and added a pop of colour via her classic red manicure.

Why it works

While head-to-toe leather might sound like a hard look to pull off, Holmes shows us that it's all about the fit. Her wide-leg trousers and baggy shirt give the look a relaxed feel, while the metallic pointed heels add a touch of elegance. Her ensemble can translate to other materials, too. From denim to silk, velvet to corduroy – no matter what full look you're wanting to wear, take style cues from Holmes and keep things baggier for the same effect.

Why it's a forever classic

Classic, well-crafted leather pieces never go out of style. Whether it's a leather trouser, shirt or coat, investing in a timeless piece is well worth your money and will work hard in your wardrobe for years to come. As shown by Holmes, a leather look is perfect for trans-seasonal dressing – and the pieces can be worn all together or styled separately, layered up with a cosy knit, or your go-to winter coat.

Where to shop it

If you want to make the investment in a leather piece like Holmes, ensure to keep it minimal and fuss-free. Loewe offers good luxury options, while Cos, Arket and Ganni are good for more affordable pieces. If you're looking for a vegan version, then Nanushka is a brand to note.

How to style it

A good pair of leather trousers or a chic leather shirt is the ultimate piece for wardrobe versatility – whether worn together top-to-toe like Holmes, or as separate pieces. Add a splash of colour with a bright knit or style with a white shirt for a chic workwear look that's office appropriate. Experiment with different shades or pair your shirt with a matching leather skirt and knee-high boots for a dressier feel.

Parker Posey provokes angry response after 'Insta-TransGender' Twitter post

Publish date: 2024-06-06

Fans have reacted with outrage after Parker Posey made an apparent 'trans gender' jibe on social media.

The actress and musician shared a Snapchat selfie of herself wearing cartoon sunglasses and a moustache, accompanied by the text, 'Insta-Transgender'.

However the image sparked anger among social media users who accused her of being 'uncool' and offensive.

This 'Insta-TransGender' Snapchat selfie of Parker Posey caused outrage among some fans 

Accompanying the image, Posey tweeted: 'I'm driving around all day today as a man! 

'I love the Internet and can't believe what you can do here!' 

One twitter user wrote: 'um this isn't OK in fact it's transphobic, you're already getting negative press about it so stop,' while another added: 'uncool, dude. also, very poorly timed.'

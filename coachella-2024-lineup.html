Lana Del Rey, Tyler, The Creator, Doja Cat

Publish date: 2024-05-27

Are you ready for another historic festival set? The Coachella 2024 lineup is finally revealed, and these stars are reported to perform at the festival once again.

Spanning over two weekends in April, Coachella puts on monumental headlining acts like Harry Styles, Billie Eilish, The Weeknd and Beyoncé. So it’s no wonder that people are speculating on Reddit threads and Twitter about who’s going to grace the main stage.

The festival is run by Goldenvoice who announced last year’s lineup on social media on January 10, 2023. The most popular festival in the world is packed with artists who cover so many genres and perform in the heat of the desert in Indio, California. 2023’s lineup included Gorillaz, Björk, Rosalia, Four Tet, Skrillex, and Fred Again.., and Bad Bunny.

So who’s on the Coachella 2024 lineup? Read more to find out.

  • Who is headlining Coachella 2024?

    The Coachella Headliners are Lana Del Rey, Tyler, The Creator, and Doja Cat. No Doubt will also perform as a special reunion. The headliners were predicted by HITSDailyDouble who have predicted the headliners for the desert festival for the last couple of years.

    Lana Del Rey performed at Coachella in 2014 and was slated to headline the festival in 2020, when it was canceled due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Tyler, The Creator performed at the festival in 2011 as a member of the collective Odd Future, and again as a solo artist in 2015 and 2018.

    Dua Lipa was rumored to be in talks to headline this year, but backed down due to scheduling conflicts. Shakira’s team also were vetting for her to be a headliner for the fest, but Coachella reportedly passed on her.

  • Who headlined Coachella 2023?

    The Coachella headliners for 2023 were BLACKPINK, Bad Bunny, and Frank Ocean. However, after backing out of Weekend 2, Blink-182 replaced Frank Ocean as a headliner.

    Frank Ocean canceled his Weekend 2 set at Coachella 2023. Frank Ocean’s reps told Rolling Stone in a statement that after suffering a leg injury at the festival during the first week of Coachella, a doctor advised that Ocean not perform the following week due to two fractures and a sprain in his left leg. “It was chaotic. There is some beauty in chaos. It isn’t what I intended to show but I enjoy being out there and I’ll see you soon,” Ocean said in a statement provided by his rep.

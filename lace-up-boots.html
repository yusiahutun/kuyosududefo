The lace-up boots we're loving this winter

Publish date: 2024-05-08

We earn a commission for products purchased through some links in this article.

Ganni x Dr. Martens

Ganni x Dr. Martens

This season, beloved Scandi favourites Ganni have reimagined the legendary Dr Martens boot. This limited-edition launch pays homage to the original design, with tell-tale yellow stitching complemented by a chunky logo on the tongue.

True to the brand's planet-friendly ethos, they're made from recycled materials, too.

Proenza Schouler

Proenza Schouler

Credit: Courtesy

Proenza Schouler's ankle boot is made in Italy from supple suede. A casual style, it's a perfect weekend boot and will look excellent styled with your favourite blue denim jeans.

Prada

This Prada combat boot balances fashion and practicality seamlessly. The upper body is made from a technical nylon and leather panelling. Finished with a chunky sole, the design is complete with a mini Prada coin purse round the ankle.

Aeyde

Dr Martens

Dr Martens

A label instantly recognisable by its tag and trademark stitching, the brand is known for its quality, iconic designs that can be worn for a lifetime.

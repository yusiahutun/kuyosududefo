Did Nicole Kidman have plastic surgery?

Nicole Kidman's plastic surgery in Lioness gossip has persisted for several years. Nicole's fans and critics have speculated that she might have undergone a wide range of non-surgical and surgical procedures to enhance her appearance.

Nevertheless, the actress has, on several occasions, denied plastic surgery rumours. In 2008, Nicole attributed her flawless complexion to a healthy lifestyle.

To be honest, I am completely natural I have nothing in my face or anything.

She added:

I wear sunscreen, and I don’t smoke. I take care of myself. And I’m very proud to say that.

However, during her interview with La Repubblica, an Italian newspaper in 2013, she confessed she tried Botox briefly but disliked the outcome. She said,

No surgery for me. I did try Botox, unfortunately, but I got out of it and now I can finally move my face again.

The Northman actress is married to Keith Lionel Urban, an Australian-American country singer, guitarist, and songwriter. The duo met in January 2005 at a Hollywood event honouring Australians in Los Angeles and started dating a few months later.

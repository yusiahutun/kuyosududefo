How Many Bling Cups Does J.Lo Own? She's Not Sure

Publish date: 2024-02-08

Jennifer Lopez isn't afraid to admit that she has a bit of an obsession—with her custom Swarovski-encrusted reusable bling cups.

In a new video made in collaboration with Coach, Lopez interviews herself, ending the quick Q&A asking, "How many bling cups do you actually have?"

The "Waiting for Tonight" singer laughs before answering, "Um … you don't want to know."

"No, you don't know," Lopez, the interviewer, responds. "You have so many that you actually do not know. It's actually kind of crazy and pathetic."

The superstar regularly shows off her bling cup collection on social media, often having new ones made to commemorate special occasions, such as her 2020 Super Bowl performance alongside Shakira, the 2021 presidential inauguration, and her new Netflix film, The Mother.

Lopez's custom vessels are crafted by "celebrity blinger" Teneka Moore of TaylorMade Bling Embellishments; she, too, often shares her special creations for the singer on social media.

"Her manicurist at the time reached out and was like, 'Hey, I want to get a birthday gift for a celebrity and I want to get a Starbucks cup. She loves bling and she loves coffee, so this gift will be perfect,'" Moore told Entertainment Tonight about how the relationship began.

"I couldn't have asked for a better blessing. It's been so crazy," she continued. "Ever since then, she orders a cup for every event, every promotion that she does. Any movie, anything that she does, she commemorates it with a cup. It's awesome."

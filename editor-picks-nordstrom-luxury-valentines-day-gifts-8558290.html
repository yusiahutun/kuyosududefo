I Found the 10 Best Designer Valentines Day Gifts at Nordstrom From $38

Publish date: 2024-03-14

February is my favorite month of the year. I know we’ve all been coming down from the Barbie pink surge, but February is another excuse to lean into all things pink and red. While Valentine’s Day is only one day,  I use the whole month to love myself the only way I know how — by treating myself at my favorite retailer, Nordstrom. 

Fortunately for me, Nordstrom curated a Luxe Valentine’s Day Gift storefront filled with designer goodies, including bags from Coach, Coperni, and Kate Spade, fragrances from Tom Ford and Parfums de Marly, and jewelry from Gucci and David Yurman. I rounded up the 10 best finds from the luxury storefront that I’m eyeing for myself ( but hopefully my boyfriend beats me to it).

Coperni Mini Heart Patent Faux Leather Tote

It’s the month of love, so naturally I’m gravitating towards everything red and heart-shaped — like this Coperni Mini Heart Patent Faux Leather Tote. I love the innovative use of the heart curve as the strap, and the sleek faux leather with the pop of cherry red would pair perfectly with a little black dress for V-day dinner plans. The tote is on the smaller side, making it ideal for holding your immediate essentials like your lipstick, phone, and wallet. Plus, the strap is just long enough that it can be worn over the shoulder. 

Comme des Garçons Play x Converse Hidden Heart High Top Sneaker

I used to want these Comme des Garçons Play x Converse high top sneakers so bad when they were first released back when I was in high school. I can confidently say that feeling hasn’t changed after I stumbled across them again for the first time in years at Nordstrom. The sneaker, which features a playful heart graphic, comes in sizes 5 through 10 and features Converse’s classic high top lace-up design. You can snag them in four colors, including black, white, gray, and blue. I’d pair these with baggy jeans, an oversized sweatshirt, and a men’s bomber jacket for effortless street styling, or leather pants and a matching sweater for a more chic outfit. 

Gucci Trademark Heart Chain Bracelet

The way to my heart is with jewelry — more specifically, this Gucci Trademark Heart Chain Bracelet. It’s got all the components of what makes a jewelry piece timeless. Made from sterling silver, it’s dainty and minimal, featuring a simple lobster clasp closure and a logo engraved heart charm. It also comes with a soft pouch for safe storing.

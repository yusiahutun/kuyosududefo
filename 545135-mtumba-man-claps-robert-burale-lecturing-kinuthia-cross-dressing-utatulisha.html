Mtumba Man Claps back at Robert Burale after Lecturing Kinuthia for Cross Dressing: &quot;Utatulisha?&quot;

Publish date: 2024-06-23
  • Robert Burale has sparked mixed reactions after calling out TikToker Kevin Kinuthia for always dressing as a woman
  • However, Mtumba Man came to the TikToker's defence and told off Burale, saying that they were hustling
  • Some social media users reminded Mtumba Man not to compare himself to Kinuthia as he was only crossdressing to sell women's clothes

Content creator Mtumba Man has taken on renowned preacher Robert Burale for calling out TikToker Kevin Kinuthia for always dressing as a woman.

The man of God had questioned why Kinuthia was wearing dresses and twerking on social media despite him being a man.

He also cautioned host Oga Obinna not to let the TikToker come near him dressed as a female, adding that Kinuthia needed to be told the truth.

Mtumba Man tells Burale reason for crossdressing

However, Mtumba man took to his TikTok and shared a video dancing, dressed in a red skirt and floral top.

He captioned the video, responding to Burale while asking him if he would feed them now that he wants men to stop dressing as women.

"Burale does not understand that we are looking for 'flour'. If I remove my skirt, will he feed me and Kinuthia? And the way I respected you," he said.

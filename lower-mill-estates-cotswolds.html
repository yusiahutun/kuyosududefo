A nature reserve in the heart of the Cotswolds

Publish date: 2024-08-06

The UK's wildest corners are ripe for exploration, and a host of sensitively-designed holiday estates, nestled in amongst our natural landscapes are making it that much easier. Lower Mill Estate, set in the Cotswold Water Park, is one such development, comprising 600 acres, eight lakes, two rivers and award-winning lodging to rent for short stays via Habitat Escapes.

This is a corner of the world for breakfast in bed, a brisk walk to spot birds of all descriptions and a hearty Sunday roast.

Nature and its preservation is at the heart of this Cotswolds landmark – it is built around a large and diverse nature reserve supporting a host of protected species – and it is amongst this nature that guests spend most of their time.

Lower Mill Estates/Habitat Escapes

Activities include boating or fishing on the scenic lakes, walking or cycling, and exploring the woodlands. Keen eyes may catch resident beavers and otters, or the house martins, nightingales and warblers that breed at the estate every year. After a muddy walk, the spa offers a touch of luxury with massages, beauty treatments and an outdoor eco-pool, naturally filtered by water lilies.

The on-site restaurant, Balihoo, is a relaxed, pasture-to-plate establishment with an all day menu catering to the large groups that descend upon it of an evening.

It is exclusively for the use of Lower Mill Estate homeowners and holidaymakers – so spaces are almost always available – whether you pop in for an early morning Eggs Benedict, fill up on a hearty lunch, or while away an evening feasting on fresh local produce and drinking good wine in its sustainably-designed surrounds.

Stone-baked pizzas are available from an adjacent outdoor pizza shack – the perfect way to refuel post-walk – providing an utterly tempting smell as you wander past. Whilst the innovative BBQ box delivers pre-prepared meat and vegetables ready for your outdoor grill.

For evenings spent in your rental home lazing in front of your log fire, Balihoo offer a private dining option, delivering a piping hot selection of fine food ready for feasting.

The menu changes seasonally, making Balihoo – and Lower Mill Estate generally – a veritable foodie destination.

Lower Mill Estates/Habitat Escapes

Left: The Lower Mill Estate spa, Right: Dinner at on site restaurant, Balihoo

Rental properties are architecturally adventurous yet sustainably minded, with completed phases of the estate deemed Excellent by the Building with Nature accreditation scheme. Many homes are perched lakeside, complete with boathouses and glass frontages that offer spectacular views, whilst others sit near private footpaths. Early risers are sure to catch a glimpse of the sun from the balconies that wrap around the first floor of most homes.

The design ethos continues indoors, where rooms are modern but cosy, furnished with classic Scandinavian design and thoughtful finishing touches like wood burners for cosy nights in.

Lower Mill Estates/Habitat Escapes

Where is it

Lower Mill Estate is located in Somerford Keynes, Gloucestershire.

How to book

2-6 bedroom holiday accommodation sleeping up to 12 people (and dogs) are available all year round. Book a stay at the Lower Mill Estate via Habitat Escapes.

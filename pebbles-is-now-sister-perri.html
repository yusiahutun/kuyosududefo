Pebbles Is Now Sister Perri

Publish date: 2024-05-05

Pebbles, a former R&B singer who also discovered TLC when she was married to Antonio "L.A." Reid, has become Sister Perri, the founder of Women of God Changing Lives Through Christ. The Atlanta-based ministry casts demons and illness out of the afflicted attendees during her regular Tuesday night "healing services," reports the Atlanta Journal-Constitution. Pebbles' real name is Perri Nixon.

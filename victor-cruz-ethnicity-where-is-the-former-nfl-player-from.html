Where Is The Former NFL Player From?

Publish date: 2024-03-12

Victor Cruz has mixed ethnicity and grew up in a Spanish household. The 2011 Super Bowl champion Cruz’s mother has Puerto Rican roots, and his father came from an African-American background.

After his dad passed away in 2007, the former New York Giants wide receiver was brought up by a single mother. Cruz is native to Paterson, New Jersey, with his father being a retired firefighter. 

After going undrafted in the 2010 NFL Draft, a scout named Chris Pettit recommended Victor to the Giants. He told the Giants they wouldn’t regret giving Cruz a chance.  

In his six seasons with the Giants, Cruz played 70 games, totaling 4,549 receiving yards and 25 touchdowns. Cruz won the Super Bowl XLVI with Eli Manning leading the offense and the New Jersey native being his top target. 

Victor Cruz Ethnicity: Where Is The Former NFL Player From?

The former NFL player Victory Cruz comes from a family with mixed ethnicity. His father, Mike Walker, was a man of African-American background, and his mother, Blanca Cruz, comes from Puerto Rican heritage.

Victor’s father, Mike Walker, was a retired Paterson, New Jersey firefighter and committed suicide in 2007. In a 2015 documentary, I Am Giant: Victor Cruz, the former NFL player talked about his father’s demise. 

In the documentary, Cruz broke down while talking about his father. He said his father’s demise kept a chip on his shoulder and made him focus on his craft.

Raised by his single mother, Blanca, Victor grew up in a Spanish household. In an interview, Victor said when he was a kid, his fashion sense was influenced by his mom’s side family.

But as a teenager, the influence of his African-American background started catching up. His mother, Blanca, worked at the Benjamin Moore customer service department and worked hard to put food on the table. 

During a conversation with AQ, Cruz said his mother would always find a way to surprise him, and if he wanted to wear something special, she would always figure out a way to get it for him on the holidays.

Victor Cruz Partner: Who Is The NFL Player Dating?

Earlier this year, Victor Cruz and a mystery woman enjoyed a downtime in Barbados. In his interview with The New York Post, the former NFL star didn’t confirm his relationship status.

He only referred to the mystery woman as a “lovely lady.” On his Instagram, the one-time Super Bowl Champion hasn’t shared any picture that would give hints about his relationship. 

Victor was previously engaged to Elaina Watley, with whom he shares his daughter, Kennedy. The two began dating in 2008 and welcomed their daughter in 2012. 

The wide receiver proposed to his longtime girlfriend in 2014 during a baby dedication ceremony for his daughter. The proposal video was later shared on the internet, with Cruz saying he was nervous before he dropped the bomb.  

But their engagement only lasted a year, with Elaina finding out Victor cheated on her with multiple women. In his interview with E!, Cruz said his toxic relationship with Elaina made it harder for them to co-parent their daughter positively. 

After breaking up with Elaina, Cruz dated actress Karrueche Tran. Victor and Karrueche began dating in 2017 but went their separate ways in February 2021. 

In an episode of The Pivot Podcast, Cruz said it was nothing malicious, and they felt they needed to do things separately. 

On his Instagram, Cruz has shared several photos and videos with his daughter, Kennedy. The father-daughter duo can be seen doing photoshoots for various brands. The two also enjoy playing golf, with Victor sharing a sweet photo of them on Father’s Day.

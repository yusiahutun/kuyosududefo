Ebanie Bridges' raunchiest Instagram snaps - from ring girl sandwich to risky nude

Publish date: 2024-05-16

Ebanie Bridges is set to make her long-awaited return to boxing for her first IBF bantamweight title defence against Shannon O'Connell.

Bridges has caught the eye both in and out of the ring in recent years after ditching her careers as a bodybuilder and teacher and turning to the sport. And her 500K plus Instagram followers have been forever grateful for that decision.

So to honour the Blonde Bomber ahead of her bout on Saturday night (December 10), Daily Star Sport is taking a look back at some of her raunchiest uploads - buckle up.

Weigh-in gear

Where else could we possibly start but with Bridges in lingerie?! Her iconic scantily-clad weigh-ins have become one of the most eagerly anticipated parts of her fight weeks.

Usually fans have to wait for her to tip the scales to see her strip off - but back in April she donned the matching set she'd worn the month before ahead of taking the IBF belt from Maria Cecilia Roman.

Ring girl sandwich

What's better than ring girls at an Ebanie Bridges fight? Ebanie Bridges with rings girls at a fight of course!

She posed for a picture with Chloe Ellman-Baker and Alissa King-Underwood while attending the Joe Joyce vs Joseph Parker event in September.

The Blonde Bomber actually used to fulfil the role herself before becoming the main attraction and defended the profession after former UFC star Khabib Nurmagomedov questioned their value in 2020.

Leeds United tattoo selfie

It's fair to say Bridges left little to the imagination when showing off her Leeds United tattoo in October. She invited fans to zoom in on the Yorkshire white rose she's had inked on her arm in honour of her beloved footy club.

But her see-through white crop top was far too much of a distraction, with one follower joking: "I zoomed in on many occasions but for some reason never on the arm. Very odd."

Crossing paths with Michelle Joy Phelps

Just a couple of days after that, Bridges crossed paths with boxing presenter and OnlyFans star Michelle Joy Phelps, with both watching on at The O2 Arena as Claressa Shields beat Savannah Marshall.

The beauties, who both sported low-cut dresses, had fans hailing "a dream combo", while a fellow crowd member turning his head in the background also caught the attention.

Posing alongside Elle Brooke

Speaking of OnlyFans, Bridges has grown close with adult star Elle Brooke in recent months after helping her train for her pro boxing debut.

And the pair set the internet alight in July as they each flaunted their assets at another of Joyce's fights, his victory over Christian Hammer at The SSE Arena.

Bridges' followers were begging her to follow in the footsteps of Phelps and Brooke and start up her own exclusive content page - and she's now finally appeased them by launching an OnlyFans.

Getting the "girls" out

The Blonde Bomber is the very definition of a tease and she was up to her old tricks with this selfie in her car. Uploading it to Insta, she wrote "summer dress for a summer day" with the cheeky hashtag "#SunsOutGirlsOut."

Those fans who weren't left speechless took the chance to banter with the bantamweight, with one writing: "What make of car is that, comes with two extra airbags." Another said: "Take the twins out for lunch? How kind of you."

Risky nude

Without a doubt Bridges' most raunchiest snap, the thong pic above nearly got her banned from Instagram. After posting it on New Year's Day she received a warning from the social media site for nudity.

She recently re-uploaded it but this time wisely added a peach emoji to cover up, recalling: "That photo nearly broke the internet this New Years and nearly got me banned."

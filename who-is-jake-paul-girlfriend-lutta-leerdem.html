Who Is Jake Paul's Girlfriend, Jutta Leerdem?

Publish date: 2024-06-17

A peek into the life of Jake Paul’s girlfriend, Jutta Leerdem. Discover how she became a part of his life after his breakup with Julia Rose.

Jake Paul, the famous YouTuber and boxing sensation, announced his new relationship with his girlfriend, Jutta Leerdem, on Twitter in April 2023. The news came months after Jake ended his relationship with Julia Rose. 

To make it official, Jake posted a picture of himself and Leerdem holding hands while jogging and added a quirky caption saying, “I’m Dutch now.”

Who Is Jake Paul?

Jake Joseph Paul is an American professional boxer and social media personality. He was born in Ohio on January 17, 1997, to his parents, Pamela Ann Stepnick and realtor Gregory Allan Paul.

Jake debuted on the video-sharing app Vine and eventually made his way to the Disney Channel series Bizaardvark. But his fame has not been without controversy. Jake once faced charges of criminal trespass and unlawful assembly.

But that hasn’t stopped him from pursuing his passion for boxing. He entered the ring in 2018, winning an amateur contest against British YouTuber Deji Olatunji.

Since then, he has transitioned to the professional circuit, recording notable victories against AnEsonGib, Nate Robinson, Ben Askren, Tyron Woodley, and Anderson Silva. However, in February 2023, he had his first professional loss after a split decision made Tommy Fury the winner of their match.

Despite the ups and downs, Jake continued to push his limits, and in January 2023, he made an announcement that raised eyebrows. He revealed that he would make his professional MMA debut with the PFL.

The move left fans wondering what’s next for this ambitious personality. However, aside from boxing, Jake has a robust love life and has been in many relationships. Currently, he is with Jutta Leerdem after ending his one-year relationship with Julia Rose.

Who Is Jake Paul’s Girlfriend, Jutta Leerdem?

Jutta Monica Leerdem is a Dutch native born on December 30, 1998. She was named after the German windsurfing world champion, Jutta Müller, by her father, a windsurfing enthusiast. 

Aside from being known as Jake Paul’s girlfriend, Leerdem is a professional speed skater who excels in sprint events. Jake Paul is captivated by her impressive athletic abilities and stunning looks.

Jutta Leerdem Is More Than Jake Paul’s Girlfriend

Leerdam is also a student of commercial economics at the prestigious Johan Cruyff Academy of the Hanze University of Applied Sciences in Groningen. Her outstanding speed skating abilities earned her multiple accolades, including the junior world championship title in 2017 and a silver medal in the 1000m event at the 2022 Winter Olympics.

In 2018, Leerdem turned professional and joined Team IKO, where she shined as a skater. That year, she won the ISU Junior World Cup competition in 1000m and 1500m events and was also named Dutch junior sprint champion.

In 2020 and 2023, Leerdem won the 1000m world championship and the gold medal at the World Sprint Championships in 2022.

As Leerdem continues to rise both on and off the ice, it’s clear that she is a talented and accomplished young woman with a bright future ahead.

How Jake Paul Met Jutta Leerdem

The ice skater and Jake met on April 3, 2023, after connecting via Instagram a few months prior. Jake wasted no time revealing the relationship and making them social media official.

While Jake appears happy and enamored with his new love, he has a history of brief and broken relationships. His last relationship lasted a year, with many breaks in between.

Leerdem’s relationships have lasted longer than the YouTuber’s. The ice skater dated Dutch speed skater Koen Verweij from 2017 until August 2022, when they announced their breakup.

Jake’s Unsteady Relationship With His Ex-Girlfriend, Julia Rose

Jake’s one-year relationship with Rose began in February 2020. Julia Rose is the Instagram sensation who made headlines during the 2019 World Series for flashing the TV behind home plate. But that was just the beginning of her fame.

She’s also a YouTube star, and she has millions of followers across social media platforms. Jake and Rose hit it off after meeting on the set of Jake’s music video for “These Days.” Their relationship had a lot of issues and was always the subject of fan discussions and media reports. 

Their dramatic breakups made headlines several times. But December 2022 marked the end for the couple when Jake shared a picture on social media with a caption that left no room for doubt – he had chosen the wrong Rose all along.

Now, Leerdem bears the title of Jake Paul’s girlfriend, and with their relationship blooming, one can only hope the boxer chose the right ‘Rose.’ However, irrespective of how it turns out, Jake Paul and Jutta Leerdem will not be the first celebrities to have a short-lived relationship.

10 best apps like Wonolo that help to get local jobs instantly

Publish date: 2024-02-15

Finding work online today is easier than in previous years. With Wonolo, you can find local companies or individuals who will hire you instantly regardless of your CV. The job categories available on the Wonolo app are either temporary or part-time. The app allows you to pick up shifts at your convenience and does not conflict with your schedule. But are there other similar apps like Wonolo that can help you get local jobs instantly and get paid within hours?

Apps like Wonolo allow you to find steady and flexible work that will pay you instantly after completion. The applications facilitate the connection between you and reliable individuals or enterprises, guaranteeing you are not subjected to fraudulent activities.

The apps have security measures that ensure you receive due compensation for your commendable efforts. You can also use the app to look for diverse local employment opportunities, encompassing warehousing, cleaning, merchandising, driving, and food production.

Apps like Wonolo that can help you get local jobs instantly

If you have used Wonolo for a while and want to change, an excellent app that you can use is Instawork. Like Wonolo, Instawork will help you find jobs that fit your schedule. According to Vital Dollar, these are the best apps like Wonolo and Instawork:

1. Snagjob

Snagjob is one of the apps that are better than Wonolo. It is an app to help you locate part-time and full-time employment opportunities. It facilitates connections between job seekers and potential employers across various industries, such as retail, customer service, and hospitality.

Snagjob is a better alternative to Wonolo because it only provides hourly jobs, making searching for a job that fits your schedule easier. Also, Snagjob caters to a specific demographic or labour force.

In other words, Snagjob targets students, teens, or anyone who needs flexible hours. Its application process is straightforward, and an applicant does not need a CV because you only have to take the employee personality quizzes and get matched with jobs that are right for you.

2. Job Today

Job Today is an online job search tool and one of the best apps similar to Wonolo. It offers a platform where job seekers can find local jobs, and employers can hire quality candidates for various positions. Job Today works by allowing you to first search for your preferred job.

The app then alerts you for work in your respective field. This app does not require candidates to submit lengthy CVs or cover letters. Instead, you can still find a well-paying gig without any paperwork by instantly chatting with a potential employee.

3. TaskRabbit

TaskRabbit is one of the best Wonolo competitors. This app's uniqueness is that it allows jobseekers to find simple handyperson and errand jobs. These jobs require no CV to apply.

TaskRabbit has jobs for skilled workers. You can find furniture assembly, cleaning, mounting, and moving jobs. Alternatively, you can deliver groceries, become a personal assistant, and research on behalf of an individual.

4. Thumbtack

Thumbtack is one of the best Wonolo alternatives for finding odd jobs without a CV. The platform only allows skilled individuals to apply for jobs they know best. You will get work on this app based on the skills you provide.

Thumbtack is the best app to find landscaping, mowing, home improvement, house cleaning, house design, moving, and painting projects. You can also find work as a DJ, fitness coach, and contractor.

5. JobStack

JobStack is the best application for discovering flexible employment opportunities that align with your skillset. It offers many temporary jobs and gigs readily available in your area. You must also add your skills and location to allow JobStack to match you with a potential employer.

6. Indeed Flex

Indeed Flex is a unique app that allows you to find temporary jobs. It caters to individuals who only want to work part-time or want to engage in a side hustle. It gives job seekers lots of freedom. For example, you can control and choose where, when, and how you want to work.

Once you get the app, you must complete your profile and get verified. Then, you start by searching for work and booking available positions within your locality. After completing your work, you will get paid based on the predetermined rate.

7. JobGet

JobGet offers one of the fastest ways to get a job online. It promises to help job seekers get a job near you within 24 hours. JobGet has different kinds of jobs. You can search for full-time, part-time, contract, gig, internship, side jobs, freelance work, and flex jobs.

8. Shiftsmart Partner

Shiftsmart Partner is an app that allows you to find flexible jobs near you. It has many jobs in different categories to ensure you only apply for a job that fits your schedule. You can get jobs in the retail, hospitality, and convenience industries.

Angi is a job search app designed for home improvement professionals. The platform allows potential employers to hire home service professionals. If you understand how to improve a home, Angi is the app you need to get hired.

Blake Lively Wears a Red Coat at Fashion Week

Publish date: 2024-06-05

In case you didn't know that today is Valentine's Day, style maven Blake Lively is here to remind you in a very fashionable way. During the actress' appearance at New York Fashion Week this morning—her first of the season—she was festively dressed from head to toe. She attended the Michael Kors show donning a bright red patent leather coat by the designer and white pumps adorned with big red hearts.

Lively also borrowed a style tip from the guys and wore a white oxford and matching tie underneath her coat. (Tapping menswear elements is nothing new for Blake, however. Remember the time she stepped out in a full, plaid three-piece suit?)

With full Lorraine Schwartz jewelry (diamond rings on her fingers, and a stunning 18-karat gold and white diamond serpent ear crawler paired with smaller studs in her ears), a structured handbag in tow and her honey-blonde hair swept to the side, the look came together perfectly.

Splash News

Despite the romantic holiday, Lively didn't show up with her husband, Ryan Reynolds. Instead, perhaps in an extended Galentine's celebration, she arrived with fellow actress Emily Blunt (whose husband, John Krasinski, was also M.I.A.). The two walked hand-in-hand as they entered the venue.

Getty Images

The two also sat together in the front row and posed for a few more joint photo opps.

Unlike Lively, Blunt opted for a blue and green color palette, mixing prints with a striped tee, floral midi skirt and color-blocked sandals. She did, however, sport red in her bold lip color.

Getty Images

So far, this marks the first look of the day for Lively. Let's see if she ends up wearing seven again.

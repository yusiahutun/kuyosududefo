Kristin Davis cuts a youthful figure in candid new post

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Kristin Davis shared an end-of-summer post to her fans on Tuesday, cutting a youthful figure as she relaxed poolside.

In a candid message to her 1.2million followers, the 57-year-old And Just Like That star revealed what she's been up to over the past few months and expressed hope that her fans were all 'hanging in there.'

'Hope you have all had some fun and relaxation this summer,' the mother-of-two wrote, before revealing: 'I am most grateful to have had so much time with my kids and my parents!'

Peaceful: Kristin Davis, 57, looked peaceful as lounged by the pool wearing a one-piece swimsuit with a floral tank top and black skirt which showcased her toned arms and legs

Davis, who adopted her daughter Gemma, 12, in 2011 and son Wilson, four, in 2018, also revealed that she had been simply too busy to share more candid moments from summer.

She explained: 'If I was more together I would make a reel of everything that happened over the summer - but gosh I can't keep up with the reels and whatnot , so here is a pic and a wish that all of you are hanging in there and taking some time to relax a bit.'

In the photo, the former Melrose Place star wore a floral swimsuit with a black skirt,  which showcased her toned arms and legs.

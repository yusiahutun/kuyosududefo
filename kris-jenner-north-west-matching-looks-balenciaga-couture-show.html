Kris Jenner &amp; North West Coordinate Looks in Balenciaga Front Row

Publish date: 2024-09-17

Kim Kardashian's family members arrived in Paris with her this week to support her appearance in Balenciaga's couture show.

This morning, the SKKN founder was among the personalities who walked the runway in Demna's futuristic designs—and the whole time, her mom and eldest daughter were sitting front row.

Kris Jenner and North West were spotted entering the venue in matching black Balenciaga ensembles. The momager wore a silky black cape dress with a mock turtleneck and matching Balenciaga sunglasses. North, meanwhile, looked cool in a faded black long-sleeved band T-shirt, dark ripped jeans with white and red stripes on the sides, and massive black-and-silver platform shoes from the Balenciaga x Crocs collaboration. She accessorized with two beaded necklaces and black Balenciaga sunglasses.

In a conversation with BAZAAR.com earlier this week, Jenner said she is incredibly busy helping manage her children's skyrocketing careers and businesses, but she always finds a way to spend time with her 11 grandkids. "Oh, my goodness. I cannot get enough of those little cuties," she said. "I love all of them absolutely equally, and I'm learning something from them every single day."

The stylish grandmother-granddaughter duo were seen excitedly watching the show from their front-row seats next to rapper Offset. They smiled as Kardashian passed them on the runway in her skintight black catsuit, long skirt, and icy-blonde do.

Jacopo M. Raule//Getty Images

Other celebrities who walked the show in Demna's jaw-dropping designs were actress Nicole Kidman, pop star Dua Lipa, legendary supermodel Naomi Campbell, and model Bella Hadid.

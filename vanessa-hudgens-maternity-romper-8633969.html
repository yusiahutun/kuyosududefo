See Vanessa Hudgens's Maternity Romper

Publish date: 0001-01-01

The Queen of Coachella herself, Vanessa Hudgens, had to sit out the music fest this year, but it was for a good reason — she's pregnant. Instead of desert-ready fashion slays, she shared a much more toned-down OOTD over the weekend, offering up a mirror selfie showing her growing baby bump in a skin-tight romper. Hudgens posted an Instagram carousel that showed her in a tan-colored playsuit. She paired the neutral-colored one-piece with a colorful floral-print hat and headphones as well as long, pointy red nails and gold and diamond rings.

The selfie also showed off a textured phone case and a dangling phone charm. She had minimal makeup on in the snapshots, though she did have lush-looking lashes and cool, rosy-colored lipstick. Other images in the post included cacti and flowers.

"Spring has sprung," she captioned the shots.

While some moms-to-be go flowy and flouncy with their maternity fashion, Hudgens has been sticking to a body-con M.O. In an Instagram post on Apr. 9, she shared a date-night look that included another skin-tight look that brought together a brown curve-hugging dress and a gold bangle as well as a messy updo. Hudgens announced her pregnancy at the 2024 Academy Awards when she arrived on the red carpet in a long black gown.

Hudgens married her husband, baseball shortstop Cole Tucker, in 2023, just months before sharing the news of her pregnancy. It's her first child, but it's already set to be in good company. Hudgens's High School Musical co-star, Ashley Tisdale, is also pregnant. Tisdale, who is 38, has a 3-year-old daughter named Jupiter Iris, whom she shares with her husband, music producer Christopher French.

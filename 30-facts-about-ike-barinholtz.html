Ike Barinholtz was born on February 18, 1977, in Chicago, Illinois.

From a young age, Barinholtz had a passion for comedy and actively participated in school theater and improv groups.

Barinholtz gained recognition for his comedic skills when he joined the cast of “MADtv” in 2002.

In addition to acting, Barinholtz has showcased his talent as a writer, penning scripts for several television shows and films.

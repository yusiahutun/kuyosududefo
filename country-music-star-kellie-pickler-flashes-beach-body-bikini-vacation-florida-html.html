Kellie Pickler is the perfect example of what competing on Dancing With The Stars does to a body.

The 27-year-old wasn't shy about posting bikini photos to her Twitter account as she enjoyed a beach vacation in Rosemary, Florida this week.

Presumably enjoying her downtime with husband Kyle Jacobs, the stunning star didn't have an inch to pinch as she paraded her toned figure in a tiny two-piece.

Babe watch! Kellie Pickler looked amazing in her tiny watermelon and white tie-dye string bikini as she hit the beach in Rosemary, Florida on September 4

She simply captioned the happy pic in which she is grinning from ear to ear with '#BeachBum :-)'.

The country music star has been living it up at fellow musician Joe Leathers' beachfront property in Walton County and she made sure to thank her friend for his generosity on Twitter.

'Thanks @joeleathers for letting us enjoy a little piece of Heaven! Xo ;-) #Rosemary,' she wrote on September 4.

'Last day in Rosemary...': The country music singer made the most of her rare downtime, taking to the ocean and working on her golden tan

The 27-year-old sported a stunning golden tan as she took to the ocean in a watermelon and white tie-dye bikini that revealed plenty of cleavage.

Attention was drawn to her toned tummy thanks to a shiny silver bellybutton ring, while she also wore a delicate gold necklace.

Large silver-rimmed shades protected the make-up-free star's eyes from the blazing sun and a slick of pink lipgloss set off her bright white teeth.

'Little piece of Heaven!' The 27-year-old thanked musician pal Joe Leathers for letting her vacation at his home on the Florida coast

The American Idol favourite appeared sad to head home to Nashville, Tennessee following her relaxing holiday, tweeting on September 5, 'Last day in Rosemary...'

Kellie will likely be tuning in to the upcoming new season of DWTS when it premieres later this month, after winning season 16 with partner Derek Hough.

Is Fallout 76 cross platform in 2024? All you should know

Publish date: 0001-01-01

If you are a role-playing games (RPG) fan, you might have encountered Fallout 76. The video game was initially released in 2018 but received mixed reviews from gamers and critics. However, despite this, the RPG title was popular among players, mainly because it was the first online multiplayer in the franchise. Since its inception and subsequent improvements via updates, fans have been wondering if you can play with friends on other platforms. So, is Fallout 76 cross-platform enabled?

Fallout 76 was developed by Bethesda Game Studios and published by Bethesda Softworks. It is an instalment in the Fallout series and a prequel to previous entries. In the gameplay, players explore the open world, which has been torn apart by nuclear war, with others. The gameplay is excellent, and you can join forces with other real-world players or friends to explore and compete. Is Fallout 76 cross platform?

Unfortunately, the video game doesn't support cross platform compatibility. The lack of the attribute means that Xbox and PC players cannot play the RPG title together online. But why does such a vast title lack a feature that other competitor series have?

Gaming issues

One reason is that Fallout 76 has had issues since its release in 2018. The title was criticised for numerous technical problems, overall design, lack of gameplay purpose, and the initial absence of human non-playable characters. In addition, reviewers also questioned the quality of physical content.

History of the developers

Bethesda Games Studios, the developers of Fallout 76, have developed other titles. These titles include The Elder Scrolls series (2002–present), The Fallout series (2008–current), and Starfield (2023). The Elder Scrolls series is the franchise's best-selling, best-reviewed game. But despite its positive reception, the developers have yet to introduce cross-platform compatibility on the game.

Can PC and PS4 crossplay Fallout 76?

Unfortunately not. The title doesn't offer crossplay compatibility between PC and PS4. If you wish to play with someone on another platform, you must switch to their console of choice.

Can you play Fallout 76 with friends on Xbox?

Yes, you can. Playing the title on Xbox is easy if you are on the same platform. To play co-op with a friend, players can join their friends via the console interface or invite them to join their Fallout 76 server. Below is a step-by-step guide on how to play with friends on Xbox.

  • Launch the game on your Xbox console
  • Make sure that your friends have launched the game on their Xbox consoles
  • Long press the home button on your controller to access the guide menu
  • Scroll and select the parties and chats options
  • Start a party
  • Then choose the invite more option
  • Choose the friends you want to invite
  • Click invite, and they will receive the invitation prompt
  • To request your friends to a group, highlight their name and select invite to the team

Is Fallout 76 cross-progression?

Unfortunately, no. The title doesn't offer the chance to share your progression with another player or console. However, players who had their games on the Bethesda Launcher could transfer their games and saves to Steam. The announcement came after the game developer announced that the Bethesda Launcher would be unusable from 11 May 2022.

Changing faces: Has Jordan had a nose job?

Publish date: 0001-01-01

It might not be as obvious as her other forays into surgical enhancement. But Jordan has taken another step away from the looks nature gave her.

The glamour model turned novelist - who had her 32FF chest reduced to a less cartoonish size last month - has also been showing off a new nose shape.

The latest procedure, thought to be a simple rhinoplasty, or nose job, has taken her cosmetic surgery bill to an estimated £36,000.

She has had four operations on her breasts, as well as liposuction on her bottom and thighs.

The 29-year-old, who now prefers to be known as Katie Price, has also confessed to a fondness for regular anti-wrinkle Botox injections at £400 a time.

However, the amount she has spent is small change compared with the £30million she is thought to have earned over the years.

Harley Street surgeon Aristolos Gaitanis said: "It's obvious she's had rhinoplasty surgery. It's down to the difference in the profile.

"Her profile used to be prominent, stronger. Now it is softer and prettier - it's the most popular sort of nose surgery.

"It involves removing the bump from the top of the nose in an operation which costs about £5,000."

The mother of three flew to Los Angeles in December for a breast reduction.

She had her first bust enhancement aged 20 to increase her natural 32B chest. Three years later, she emerged as a 32FF.

The latest procedure, which brought her back down to a 32D, was a Christmas present from her husband Peter Andre, 34.

The singer is no stranger to the art of nip and tuck himself, having had a nose job in 1997.

But he has told his wife to ease up on the surgery, saying: "I don't want you ending up like Michael Jackson. I'd rather you're still with me when you're 100 than pop your clogs at 42 looking amazing because the anaesthetic finally takes its toll."

Mr Gaitanis concurred, saying: "The risks increase the more times you have the operations."

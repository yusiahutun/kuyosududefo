Prince Harry fans have 'no idea what Meghan Markle has done' - 'He looks different'

Publish date: 2024-07-17

Prince Harry and Meghan Markle enjoyed a wholesome day out this weekend attending a charity Polo game at Grand Champions Polo Club, and fans can’t notice that Harry looks considerably different.

The couple were suited and booted for the event, however, Prince Harry stripped it back in another polo cameo, wearing just a T-shirt and a cap, something you may not have seen often during his royal days. The Duke wasn’t alone, as he was joined by a Netflix camera crew, and it’s not the first time the 39-year-old has been followed by the streaming service cameras. In December 2022, he and Meghan Markle released their bombshell documentary, and he also sat down in front of the cameras for Heart of Invictus. Now, there’s another project in the pipeline.

Fans don’t know what Meghan’s doing to Harry – but they’re not complaining

It looks like the rare sight of King Charles‘ youngest son dressed down in a T-Shirt and snapback was a hit with fans, as the photos began to circulate on X, formerly known as Twitter.

“I don’t know what Meghan’s doing to her man, but he’s glowing,” wrote one, next to a picture of him smiling in the sun.

“He can finally wear T-shirts, rock his dad bod, love his wife, and dance to Shenseea whenever he wants. This man ain’t stop smiling since he left the castle,” another wrote.

A third penned: “This is a man who’s finally found what he was dreaming of/saying since a teenager: an equal loving partner, unconditional love, a partner who can both stand on her own two feet & partner with him for common projects, his own family, a home & stability.”

The Duke of Sussex has been in Florida this week to film scenes for his new Netflix show, about the world of polo. The show will be “shot primarily at the US Open Polo Championship in Wellington, Florida at The USPA National Polo Center,” per Sussex.com, and it’s not the only new show for the couple.

Archewell Productions recently announced two new nonfiction series were in production at Netflix, with Meghan releasing a show that will “celebrate the joys of cooking & gardening, entertaining, and friendship.”

The Duke’s not ditching the ‘suited and booted’ look just yet

Although Harry was seen sporting a toned-down look, he’s not ditching the smarts yet, as he and Meghan stunned at their polo appearance on Friday.

The Duchess wore a chic white dress with sunglasses, as Harry coordinated in white pants and a light grey jacket on top of a blue shirt.

They weren’t holding back on the PDA as they held hands while walking through the event, and even shared a celebratory kiss when Meghan awarded Harry a trophy for his team’s victory.

“They were very loving with each other,” an insider told PEOPLE. “Meghan looked really happy.

“They held hands as they arrived and talked to everyone there,” the source adds. “It was Harry’s night to shine and Meghan was very supportive.”

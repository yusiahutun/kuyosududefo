LBI Entertainment Launches Sports Division

Publish date: 2024-06-06

LONDON (CelebrityAccess) — Steve Kutner and Cobi Jones have joined forces with management company LBI Entertainment to launch a sports management operation that will focus primarily on soccer.

Kutner will be based out of the company's London offices and his client roster includes Frank Lampard (New York City FC), Patrick Bamford (Chelsea/Crystal Palace), Philippe Senderos (Aston Villa and Switzerland), plus coaches including Steve Bould (Arsenal), Claudio Ranieri (Leicester City), Tony Adams (Gabala), as well as former players and TV personalities Paul Ince, Ally McCoist and Ian Wright.

Jones is one of the most recognized soccer stars in the U.S. and formerly a member of the U.S. Men’s National Team. He also played 12 seasons for the LA Galaxy of Major League Soccer. Prior to that, Jones who is a member of the National Soccer Hall of Fame, played in the English Premier League for Coventry City and in Brazil for Vasco da Gama. He currently serves as a broadcaster with Fox Sports, Time Warner Cable SportNet and Pac-12 Network. – Staff Writers

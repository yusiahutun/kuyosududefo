Dakota Johnson and Chris Martin Break Up Reports, Split Rumors

Publish date: 2024-08-27
  • Dakota Johnson and Chris Martin have split up, according to reports.
  • A source allegedly close to the Coldplay frontman told The Sun that "they have gone their separate ways, which has come as a real surprise to everyone."
  • Johnson and Martin were first spotted out on a date in October 2017. They even spent Christmas 2018 as a family, along with Martin's ex-wife, Gwyneth Paltrow, and children, Apple and Moses.

Love is officially dead. According to reports, Dakota Johnson and Chris Martin have ended their relationship after more than 18 months of dating.

The Sun quotes a source as saying, "There was talk of engagement but now he says they have gone their separate ways, which has come as a real surprise to everyone."

The split is especially surprising to friends of the couple, with one source revealing, "Chris and Dakota were very easygoing and always seemed really happy together." Johnson seemed to become close to Martin's ex-wife, with Derek Blasberg sharing a photo with both actresses on Instagram, cleverly captioned, "Consciously throupling":

Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

Chris Martin and Gwyneth Paltrow announced their split in 2014.

Johnson and Martin were spotted having dinner together in October 2017, and by December 2017 it was being reported that they were "definitely dating." From there, the couple's relationship seemed to have gotten pretty serious, with the Fifty Shades of Grey actress spending Thanksgiving 2018 with Martin, Gwyneth Paltrow, their two kids—Apple and Moses—and Paltrow's new husband, Brad Falchuk.

People reported at the time, per a source with knowledge of the situation: "They’re a total modern family and had a great time celebrating together." Johnson also apparently spent the holidays with the Coldplay singer and his ex-wife. In addition, Martin and Johnson fended off false pregnancy rumors in October 2018.

35 Facts about the movie Bedknobs and Broomsticks

Publish date: 2024-07-26
Source: Disneyplus.com

Bedknobs and Broomsticks is a classic family film that has enchanted audiences for decades. Released in 1971, this beloved movie combines live-action and animation in a whimsical tale filled with magic, adventure, and heartwarming moments. Set during World War II, Bedknobs and Broomsticks follows three orphaned children who are sent to live with the eccentric Miss Eglantine Price. Little do they know that Miss Price is secretly a witch in training and embarks on a journey to find the missing pieces of a powerful spell. Along the way, they encounter talking animals, soccer-playing animated suits of armor, and even a magical bed that can fly. With its memorable characters, catchy songs, and stunning visual effects, Bedknobs and Broomsticks has become a timeless classic that continues to captivate audiences of all ages.

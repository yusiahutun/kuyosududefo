English actress Rhona Mitra landed her first major role in 1994 as Scott Wolf's illicit love interest, Holly Marie, on the TV series Party of Five. Following that, the actress appeared in a variety of roles in movies like Ali G Indahouse (2002), Sweet Home Alabama (2002), Stuck on You (2003), Highwaymen (2004), and Spartacus (2004).

Also a singer, Rhona released her debut studio album, Come Alive, in 1998, produced by musician and record producer Dave Stewart. Before that, she had already launched a single Getting Naked in 1997. 

Besides being a singer and an actress, she has also modeled for several brands, including Jones Apparel Group’s denim wear, and as Lara Croft in the video game Tomb Raider, between 1997 and 1998. 

Rhona Mitra's Bio: Age, Family Details

The actress was born as Rhona Natasha Mitra on 9th August 1976 in Paddington, London, England. Her parents are Anthony Mitra and Nora Downey. When Rhona was eight years of age, her parents separated, and they officially got divorced in 1984.

About

Colton Haynes is a 35 year old American Actor. Born Colton Lee Haynes on 13th July, 1988 in Andale, Kansas, USA and educated at Navarre High School (Florida) and Samuel Clemens High School in Schertz, TX, he is famous for Teen Wolf (2011-12), Arrow (2013). His zodiac sign is Cancer.

Willa Holland is a 32 year old American Actress. Born Willa Joanna Chance Holland on 18th June, 1991 in Los Angeles, California, USA, she is famous for her roles as Kaitlin Cooper in the Fox teen drama series The O.C., Agnes Andrews in the CW teen drama series Gossip Girl, Aqua (voice) in the PlayStation Portable video game series Kingdom Hearts, and Thea Queen/Speedy in the CW superhero action-adventure series Arrow in a career that spans 1995–present. Her zodiac sign is Gemini.

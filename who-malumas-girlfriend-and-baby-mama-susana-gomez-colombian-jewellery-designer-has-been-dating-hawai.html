Colombian-singer-songwriter Maluma has been stripping it down for Playgirl magazine, recently teasing a series of photos from his March cover shoot on Instagram.

In the pics, the “Felices Los 4” star is seen shirtless in a kitchen and posing in a half-unbuttoned pyjama set. “I’ve been [acting sexy] for almost 10 years, since I started my career,” Maluma said in the accompanying interview, adding that it’s OK to “admire someone and think that a person is sexy or that they’re hot,” even if they’re in a relationship.

Speaking of being in a relationship, Playgirl ran with the cover title “Maluma wants to be your daddy”, hinting at the fact that the star is about to embark on fatherhood in real life too.

So who’s the woman who’s stolen his heart? Here’s what we know about his low-key girlfriend and baby mama Susana Gomez …

Susana Gómez is from Colombia

Gómez and Maluma both hail from Medellin, Colombia, per People magazine, and they’re both very proud of their heritage.

Gómez’s jewellery brand, Sileo, features handcrafted pieces made in her hometown, for example. “[The brand uses] precious stones such as diamonds, Colombian emeralds, rubies, or other kinds of semi-precious stones,” according to the brand’s Instagram page.

She is an architect and interior designer

Unlike her beau, Gómez doesn’t work in the entertainment business. People reports that she in fact made a name for herself as an architect and interior designer in Colombia, and added jewellery designer to her list of skills just last year.

She hangs out with Maluma’s family

As seen in photos on social media, Gómez clearly gets along with her partner’s family. She’s been featured on the Instagram feeds of Maluma’s mum Marlli Arias, sister Manuela Londoño and manager Clara Pablo, as reported by People.

She’s very private

The creative entrepreneur maintains a low profile compared to her ultra famous partner, especially when it comes to their relationship. Nevertheless, Maluma regularly shares posts of their lovey-dovey life together on his socials, including multiple photos of Gomez’ pregnant belly in recent months as they prepared to welcome their first child together.

She’s just given birth to a baby daughter

The pair have been together since 2020 according to People magazine. On March 9, the couple welcomed their first child together, which they named Paris Londoño-Gomez.

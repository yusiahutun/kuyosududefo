Its LeAnn Rimes House!

Publish date: 2024-09-07

LeAnn Rimes and husband Eddie Cibrian bought this house in December 2012 after a few years spent renting one just down the road. They waited for a deal and got one, paying $3 million for this property on a short sale. The house is built on two acres of land and features beamed ceilings, a den and a barbecue area outdoors.

Aside from the main home, there's a separate guest house out back. There you'll also find an outdoor entertainment area that includes numerous covered porches. Scroll down for pics, then click the bottom below to see more homes of country stars.

The 12 best luxury gifts for pampered pets

Publish date: 2024-08-03

These make the pawfect Christmas gifts for our furry friends

    The breadth of luxury gift guides around the festive time of year is endless. From personalised gifts, to earrings, stocking fillers. But let’s not forget our furry family members who deserve luxury gifts this Christmas as much as we do.

    From our favourite high-end British brands like Fortnum and Mason and Barbour to fashion elites like Prada and Gucci, there are a plethora of luxury gifts out there that are just as perfect as your pet. Brands appreciate the relationship between humans and their pets and therefore often make the same designs on their coveted items pet-appropriate. Forget matching pyjamas with a partner or gal pal., matching luxury fashion with your pet is where it’s at.

    How I chose:

    Budget: Being a luxury gift guide, the pieces in this edit are more expensive than the average for the product. However, I've still found items to suit all budgets, starting from £16.50

    Design: All pieces are from coveted brands recognised for their quality and are pieces that are trend-led for right now, or are timeless. They're also all things I would absolutely use on my own pet (he is a Beagle, in case you were wondering). I've found a variety of products from collars to toys, shampoo to pet carry bags in the hope there is something to suit everybody (well, pet).

Im Buying Another Pair of Ugg Slippers While Theyre in Stock

Publish date: 2024-10-09

I get whiplash every year when the temperature fluctuates from 80-plus degrees to below 60. Over the last few weeks, I started pulling my heavier sweaters and leather jackets out of storage, but with the weather still in flux, I kept my sandals handy. But after a few freezing cold walks with my pup, I knew it was time to put Ugg Tasman Slippers back into my shoe rotation. They were constantly sold out last fall and winter, but my lovely boyfriend was able to snag a pair for me during a quick restock. And with how much I wore them last year, I’m thinking of picking up a second pair before they inevitably sell out again.

Ugg Tasman Slippers

The indoor and outdoor slippers come in black, brown, gray, green, and pink in whole sizes five through 12, but it’s recommended to size up if you wear half sizes. They feature Ugg’s buttery soft suede on the outside and a plush wool-blended inner that’s moisture-wicking while keeping your feet warm. There’s an embroidered trim around the outer edge opening, a 1-inch platform, and a grippy rubber sole that gives you great traction on slick or uneven surfaces. 

I prioritize comfort within every facet of my life, but even more so in the fall and winter. Because of my always-cold toes and chilly weather-induced fatigue, it just made sense to add the Ugg slippers to my arsenal, and now — fingers crossed — I can secure a second pair while they are still in stock. The slip-on shoe is soft, lightweight, breathable, and keeps my feet from sweating or overheating while still fitting snugly on the foot.

I also love that these are super easy to walk in — as opposed to the chunkier platform style — but I suggest sizing up if you want to wear them with socks. The fur on the inside of the shoe is quite thick at first, so they need to be worn in a bit to get the right amount of wiggle room. The slippers are easy to style and can be paired with almost anything in your closet. I’ve styled them with jeans, a chunky sweater, or a matching sweat set for a cozy look.

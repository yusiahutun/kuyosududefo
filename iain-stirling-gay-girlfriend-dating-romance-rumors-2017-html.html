Iain Stirling's romantic relationship with his girlfriend has finally been confirmed, and the news has automatically sent his gay rumors down the drain.

Today, let's take a peek at the most anticipated questions regarding his personal life, which have now been finally confirmed!

Who Is Iain Stirling's Girlfriend?

Iain Stirling met his partner Laura Whitmore at an ITV party after their mutual friend Ore Oduba from 'Strictly Come Dancing' introduced them.

The sparks flew between them, and their friendship soon turned into a beautiful romantic relationship.

At first, they attempted to keep their relationship out of the spotlight, but they could not keep their romance a secret for long. As per reports in July 2017, an insider to Sun revealed,

"They kept the relationship private to give it the best possible chance, but it’s well known in their close circle of friends how happy they are.

Friends of the boyfriend and girlfriend commented on their relationship as,

 "They're the perfect match."

The couple kept the speculations of their dating affair alive for some months until Iain's girlfriend, Laura, confirmed their relationship. She told The Sun in August 2017, during the launch of P.F. Chang's Asian Table Restaurant in London, 

''Yeah, he's my boyfriend. He's lovely.

She added,

I've got him as my screensaver. I've known him for ages. I had never watched Love Island before, but I don't need him to give me spoilers because everyone from I'm A Celebrity works on it anyway.''

Moreover, some sources revealed that they were in love with one another. The source divulged, "They are very much in love and are boyfriend and girlfriend."

According to reports, Iain and his girlfriend Laura have been dating since early 2017. Although the couple has not shared many details about their relationship, the fans are hopeful that they will share more in the future.

Iain Stirling Taking a Selfie with Girlfriend Laura Whitmore and a Friend (Source: Mirror)

However, the couple spent some months apart, as Iain is set in Scotland for Edinburgh Fringe Festival and Laura is set to be in London. She shared the same with Metro in August 2017,

'‘I’ve gone back to school. I’m doing a course at RADA, ‘I’ll be there for five weeks, then I’m off to New York in September and Los Angeles in October. I love acting. ‘I’ve caught the bug, but I’ve got so many lines to learn.’' 

Before Iain dated his girlfriend Laura, several rumors hit the media claiming Iain was gay, but the rumors went down after the couple made their relationship public.

As for the gay rumors, we cannot assume his sexuality as there has been no statement from the actor's side.

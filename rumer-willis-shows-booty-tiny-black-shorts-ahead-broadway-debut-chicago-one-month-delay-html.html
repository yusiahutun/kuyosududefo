Rumer Willis shows off her booty ahead of Broadway debut in Chicago

Publish date: 2024-03-04

After a one month delay due to injury, Rumer Willis will finally make her Broadway debut as Roxie Hart in Chicago this coming Monday.

And on Friday the 27-year-old celebrated the upcoming Broadway stint at a photo call at Sardi's restaurant in New York City.

She showed off her toned figure in tiny, booty-hugging shorts as she posed in front of posters for the show.

Rumer wore a plunging, skintight black top, which she coupled with a pair of black shorts that highlighted her pert derriere.

The tiny bottoms also showcased the star's toned pins, and she coupled them with a pair of black flats.

She covered up with a form-fitting black blazer, and showed off a coordinating black manicure for the occasion.

The daughter of Bruce Willis and Demi Moore sported her short, raven tresses styled straight, and otherwise kept her accessories simple.

She showed off a bold, smoky eye for the Chicago photo call, balancing it out with a soft, pink lipstick. 

Rumer was spotted posing at the photo call alongside Chicago's producer Barry Weissler, with the two at one point sharing a warm embrace and a kiss on the cheek.

Looking fit: The star's original debut had to be postponed because she had suffered stress fractures on her foot while on the Dancing With The Stars Live! Perfect Ten tour in June

Bold: Rumer showed off a smoky eye for the appearance, but balanced it with a soft, pink lipstick

The Dancing with the Stars reigning champion is set to begin performing as Roxie Hart in the Broadway production on September 21 after an injury resulted in a month-long delay.

She had suffered stress fractures on her foot while on the Dancing With The Stars Live! Perfect Ten tour in June, and was only recently able to return to the dance studio. 

On Thursday Rumer celebrated her upcoming debut, as she Tweeted: '#TeamRuelers coming to see me in the show dont be afraid to tell me who you guys are cause I only know most of you by your twit/IG handles [smiley face emoji].'

The day before she had apologized to fans for her delayed start, expressing how sorry she was to anyone who had bought tickets not knowing her debut had been postponed. 

In good company: The raven-haired beauty smiled as she posed alongside Chicago's producer, Barry Weissler

Can't hide her smile! The actress seemed elated to finally be preparing to begin her stint as Roxie Hart on Broadway after a month-long delay

In a statement on her instagram she said: 'I cannot even begin to express how sorry I am that you guys were not informed that my debut was postponed.' 

However, she followed that with a more upbeat post, as she shared a photograph of herself in character as Roxie.

She can be seen sporting a short, blonde wig along with a lacy, black leotard in the promotional shot, which she cheerily captioned: 'Who's ready....3 days.'

Rumer will be performing as Roxie Hart in Chicago on Broadway from September 21 to November 1.

Ta Leoni Admits She's Enjoying Single Life After Divorce From David Duchovny - Closer Weekly

Publish date: 2024-03-23

Téa Leoni is single and ready to mingle!

In a new interview with ‘MORE’ magazine, the 49-year-old actress was asked about her divorce from ex-husband David Duchovny — and she confessed she’s enjoying life on her own.

“[I’m] divorced and liking it, thank you,” she quipped to the mag.

Téa and David at the Golden Globes in 2010.

Téa wed her second husband David, 54, in 1997 after an eight-week courtship. After 17 years of marriage, the pair separated in 2012. Their divorce was finalized two years later.

Though the ‘Deep Impact’ star admitted she and David have had their ups and downs, she added they are committed to amicably co-parenting their two children, Madelaine, 15, and Kyd, 12.

“On occasion, I want to throttle him,” she said of her former hubby. “But in any real relationship with someone you love, that’s true.”

After her much-publicized split, Téa told the publication she’ll “never speak again” about her personal relationships, “especially after what it did to my kids.”

David, Madelaine, Téa and Kyd in 2008.

As for her career? The mom-of-two is choosing to focus on her new TV show ,’Madam Secretary,’ instead of worrying about growing older in the spotlight.

“Chasing youth is a war I’m not going to win,” Téa admitted to ‘MORE.’ “It’s not like I’m thrilled to turn around and catch my can in the mirror, but I can see now how much of my happiness could be a victim of trying to stay young and desirable. And it feels like peace and victory to be relieved of that burden.”

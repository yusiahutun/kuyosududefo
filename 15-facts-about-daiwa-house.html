FAQs

1. What is the history of Daiwa House?

Daiwa House was founded in 1955 by Nobuo Ishibashi and is headquartered in Osaka, Japan. The company has since grown to become one of the leading real estate and construction firms in the world.

2. What are some notable projects completed by Daiwa House?

Some notable projects completed by Daiwa House include Tokyo’s Odaiba Marine House, Tokyo Disneyland hotels, and the development of sustainable housing communities such as the Smart Plaza Tama Center.

3. How does Daiwa House prioritize sustainability?

Daiwa House is committed to sustainability and incorporates environmentally friendly practices in their projects. This includes using energy-efficient materials, implementing recycling programs, and promoting the development of eco-friendly housing.

4. What regions does Daiwa House operate in?

Daiwa House operates in Japan, as well as various regions globally. Their international operations include countries such as the United States, China, Southeast Asia, and Australia.

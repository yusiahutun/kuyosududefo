Chris Rock splits with girlfriendMegalyn Echikunwoke after four years of dating

Publish date: 2024-05-26

Chris Rock, 55, and Megalyn Echikunwoke, 36, have called it quits after four years of dating. 

The pair split a few months ago, before Rock's memorable appearance at the Oscars where he poked fun of Amazon founder Jeff Bezos, according to Page Six. 

Sources claim Rock still wasn't ready to settle down yet, following the divorce of his wife Malaak Compton-Rock, who he was married to from 1996 to 2014, with the divorce finalized in 2016.

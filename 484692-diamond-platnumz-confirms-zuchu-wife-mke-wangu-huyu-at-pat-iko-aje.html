Diamond Platnumz Confirms Zuchu Is His Wife: &quot;Mke Wangu Huyu&quot;

Publish date: 2024-05-24
  • Diamond Platnumz is madly in love with Zuchu, and their fans cannot get over the fact that he has found love
  • The singer, while speaking at an event, confirmed that Zuchu was his wife and referred to her as so
  • The Tanzanian star recently showered Zuchu with love on her birthday, noting that he will always love her

Wasafi Classic Baby boss Diamond Platnumz has confirmed that Zuchu is his wife.

Diamond Platnumz refers to Zuchu as his wife

While at a music launch, the Tanzanian star noted that he loved the song Juma Jux and his lady Zuchu had made.

"Habari za jioni mabibi na mabwana. Mimi naitwa Diamond Platnumz na siku hii ni siku ya furaha mimi kuja hapa kujiunga na mwenzangu Juma jux na yeye ni miongoni wa wasanii napenda sana kwa sababu tukianza mziki tulianza pamoja na nafurahi kuwa kila mtu anaendelea vyema sehemu tofauti tofauti."

Diamond was honoured to introduce the song that Jux had collaborated on with Zuchu.

The Tanzanian star also revealed that he and Jux were still working together behind the scenes and that he was proud of him.

The WCB boss noted that it was his personal favourite and referred to Zuchu as his wife which had the audience cheering loudly.

Shyly and almost as if he was running away, he exited the stage and left the crowd clapping.

"Lakini tumekua tukishirikiana sehemu tofauti tofauti. Na sisi kama vijana kama mfano. Nimekua hapa leo kutambulisha nyimbo yake. Nimbo hii ya juma jux katika album yake, album hii personally ni favourite yangu nyimbo hii ya ndugu yangu akimshirikisha mke wangu Zuchu."

On Zuchu's birthday recently, the singer showered her with love noting that he would never abandon her and she would always have a shoulder to lean on.

Diamond Platnumz's mum calls Zuchu daughter-in-law

On Wednesday, Diamond's mother, Sandra Kassim, celebrated Zuchu, referring to her as her daughter-in-law.

Diamond's mother shared a lovely video of the singer pampering her with love.

"Nakutakia maisha marefu yenye baraka... Ma mkwe (I wish you a long life full of blessings, my daughter-in-law) Zuhura Othman Suod @officialzuchu," she wrote.

In response, the Wasafi Record singer said:

"Asante mamaangu," attaching a love heart emoji.

Biography Timeline

Madison Reyes is an American actress and singer who rose to fame for her lead role in the Netflix series “Julie and the Phantoms. Here is a timeline of her life and career:

  • July 10, 2004: Madison Reyes was born in Brooklyn, New York.
  • 2018: Reyes auditioned for “Julie and the Phantoms” in New York City, her first-ever audition.
  • 2019: Reyes was cast as the lead character, Julie Molina, in the Netflix series “Julie and the Phantoms”. She filmed the series in Vancouver, Canada.
  • September 10, 2020: “Julie and the Phantoms” was released on Netflix, earning Reyes critical acclaim for acting and singing.
  • November 2020: Reyes released her first single, “Notice Me,” written and produced by her co-star Charlie Gillespie.
  • December 2020: Reyes performed at the virtual Christmas Day Parade for Disney Channel.
  • February 2021: Reyes was nominated for the Kids’ Choice Award for Favorite Female T.V. Star.
  • March 2021: Reyes announced that she would be releasing more music.

Madison Reyes has become a rising star in the entertainment industry, and her future looks bright as she continues to pursue her passion for acting and singing.

Meet Street Outlaws Lee Roberts! His Bio, Net Worth, Wife, Brother, Age

Publish date: 2024-09-29

Meet Lee Roberts. star of “Street Outlaws: Memphis”. He has been a strong contender and a phenomenal racer on the show. But, outside of the street racing, he is an admirable father to two boys and husband to his wife.

Learn all about Lee in this article. Here, we also cover his net worth and information on his brother.

Lee Roberts On Street Outlaws

Lee Roberts is one of the street racers appearing on “Street Outlaws: Memphis” which is led by JJ Da Boss. The show is the spin-off of the original series “Street Outlaws”. The show made its on-screen debut first in January 2018. It became an instant hit and got renewed for the second installment in October 2018.

But, the show was embroiled in a scandal. JJ Da Boss was accused of assaulting a racer couple who had been invited to film for the show. Despite the scandal, the show returned for the third season in September 2019. Following that, season 4 was released on March 23rd, 2020.

In the show, Lee is dubbed as the strategizing racer. He adapts his vehicle to cope with the specific Memphis streets he will be racing through. He drives a 1973 Nova that he has named “Night Force”. In June 2019, he won twice consecutively pocketing a $20,000 cash prize in North Carolina. In the first race, he won against Joshua Day who finished as a runner-up.

Later, in August, Lee competed against Dennis Bailey and won $10,000 in a big tire arm drop race at FreeBird Dragway in Ohio.

Other racers joining him on the show are Doughboy, Jeff James, Brian Britt, Justin ‘CARP’ Carpenter, Mike Gage AKA Mustang Mike, Dennis Bailey, Kenneth Gulley, Donn Gingrich, Jamie Otts, Jason Ainsworth, Tricia Day, and Precious Cooper.

Because of the looks of Lee Roberts and JJ Da Boss, they were rumored to be related. But there is nothing that suggests they are blood relatives.

How Much Is Lee Roberts Net Worth?

Lee Roberts has a net worth under $1 million.

Outside of his racing career, Lee is working as a manager at Hatchie River Lumber Co. Hatchie Hardwood Lumber is a hardwood concentration yard located in Pocahontas, Tennessee. The business is located 80 miles East of Memphis and 45 miles South of Jackson, TN in Hardeman County.

The company specializes in 4/4, 8′ – 14′ length hardwood lumber. They also offer Kiln Dried, Air Dried, Rough, or S2S material. On his Facebook, he has also posted about vacancies in his company in various positions.

Lee and his family are also very fond of hunting and fishing. He has also started training his sons for similar outdoor activities and he posts pictures of their kills on his socials.

The Roberts household is also involved in social awareness activities. In April 2020, Lee with his wife and two kids appeared on the FB page of Hardin County Carl Perkins Center to spread awareness related to Child Abuse. They attempted on spreading awareness and educate the community on child abuse.

Lee Roberts Wife

Coming to his marriage, Lee Roberts shared wedding vows with his wife Brittany Johnson on 2 February 2013. They have known each other since their age as a teenager. In February 2021, Lee took to his Facebook to wish his wife an anniversary.

He wrote, “I’d like to wish my wife a happy anniversary!! I can’t thank you enough babe for standing beside me and doing this crazy thing we call life and supporting me 100 percent no matter what I choose to do in life and being an awesome mom to our boys I am truly a blessed man to be able to call you my wife and Best friend I love you babe and Happy Anniversary hope you have a wonderful day.”

Lee and his wife Brittany are parents to two sons — Hayden (4 September 2013) and Brad James (15 August 2016).

Brittany hailed from Savannah, Tennessee. She studied Nursing at the University of Memphis after completing high school at Hardin County High School. She currently works as a Perioperative Nurse at Jackson-Madison County General Hospital and works as a Registered nurse at Deaconess Homecare and a Registered nurse at Magnolia Regional Health Center.

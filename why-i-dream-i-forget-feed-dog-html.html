Why do I dream I always forget to feed my dog?

Publish date: 2024-02-26

As I am sure you know recurring dreams are significant - so listen to this one and it may tell you something. It sounds like your dog is a loyal companion to you and you feel as though you may neglect her sometimes. Your dream may be a simple and concrete representation of a conscious or unconscious anxiety about her.

If you think this is the case, then you may need to pay her more attention. Although it doesn't sound like this fits. So perhaps, your dog represents someone else in your life who you are neglecting - someone loyal, a partner or a family member. On a deeper level the dream dog may be symbolising the animalistic side of your own self.

Perhaps you have not yet acknowledged the existence of an instinctive, disinhibited, animal side of your self and perhaps this part of you is feeling neglected and under nourished - like your dog in your dream. Maybe, most of the time you don't even mind about this undeveloped part of yourself, as represented by the non-plussed dog following you around the kitchen.

But it seems from the recurrence of this dream that this part of you is calling out to be nurtured. When nurturing happens I suspect that the dream will stop recurring.

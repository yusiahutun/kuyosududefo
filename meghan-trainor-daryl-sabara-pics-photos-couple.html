The Nantucket, Massachusetts native soared into fame when she released her first big hit “All About That Bass” in 2014. Her debut body positive track instantly became a fan-favorite, rapidly climbing Billboard’s Hot 100 chart. 

The “I Love Me” singer doesn’t just have a lot of love for her body and herself either — but also has much love for her husband Daryl Sabara. If his name doesn’t sound familiar, his face might look familiar. On February 14, 2019 (only the most romantic day of the year), the singer-songwriter dropped a music video on YouTube entitled “Marry Me.” Naturally, American actor Sabara starred in the video and can be seen standing at the altar in a reimagined wedding scene. 

Trainor and Sabara married each other IRL on December 22, 2018 in a classic Los Angeles ceremony consisting of 100 close friends and family members.

Aquarius 2024 Horoscope: Stepping into Your Power

Publish date: 2024-09-25

You’re a voice for your generation and your Aquarius 2024 horoscope is proof that you’re beginning to step into the role you were born to play. While this new year is just the start of a long and beautiful journey, it’s also revealing the themes that will carry you into the most meaningful eras of your life.

Throughout 2023, you probably started the process of making major strides toward your own self-actualization and independence, thanks to Pluto’s temporary foray into Aquarius. Now that Pluto is re-entering Aquarius from January 20 to September 1—and again on November 19—you’re settling into this new chapter even more deeply. Pluto will spend the next 20 years reconstructing your brand, your identity, and your sense of self, inspiring you to prioritize your own personal power above all things. However, you may need to allow certain things to die before something better can be reborn. Pluto is, after all, the planet of death and rebirth. Rise from the ashes like the phoenix you are.

In the past, you may have felt like you couldn’t be fully honest about your thoughts and feelings, but 2024 is going to change that. Once the North Node of Destiny joins forces with Chiron—the wounded healer—on February 19, it will activate your voice like never before, encouraging you to speak truth into your third house of communication and close friends. Remove the muzzle you unconsciously placed over your mouth. As a lunar eclipse in Libra blasts off in your ninth house of expansion and philosophy on February 19, you’ll feel the wind beneath your wings as you embrace a more open, understanding, and enlightened perspective of reality. With this comes even more game-changing conversations, especially as a total solar eclipse in Aries rises in your communication sector on April 8. Prepare for changes in your relationship dynamics—especially with siblings and close friends—to unfold.

In order for you to feel strong enough to take on the world, you need to have a sacred space to return to at the end of a long day. And as Jupiter forms a conjunction with Uranus in your fourth house of home and family on April 20, you may find the inspiration to create the household you’ve always dreamed of having. You may notice many serendipitous changes unfolding in your personal life during this time and all of them will be exciting, enlivening, and encouraging. However, as Jupiter enters Gemini on May 25, it will spread the seeds of love across your fifth house of creativity and romance, inspiring you to express yourself to the fullest for the remainder of 2024.

You may notice changes in your financial status beginning to unfold by the arrival of fall. When a lunar eclipse in Pisces shakes things up in your second house of money and self-esteem on September 17, you may be taking stock of your bank account, your personal possessions, and all the items you place value in. This could lead to a healthy raise if you’ve been playing your cards right. And by the time a solar eclipse in Libra dawns on November 25, you may find yourself embarking on a new adventure in your ninth house of wisdom and open-ended expansion. By the end of the year, you’ll probably travel to new places and be thrown into new experiences that expand your horizon.

However, the end of the year could bring rise to long-standing conflicts in your one-on-one relationships. As Mars in Leo stations retrograde in your seventh house of partnerships as of December 6, it will rake over the coals in your most intense unions, revealing where anger could be used and communicated more effectively. Don’t let your ego get the best of you.

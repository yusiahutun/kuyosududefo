Heidi Klum and Tyra Banks Reunited at Mariah Carey Concert

Publish date: 2024-08-12

How many legends can you fit onto one stage? While this may seem like a trick question, the answer, apparently, is three. Over the weekend, supermodels, besties, and OG Victoria's Secret Angels Heidi Klum and Tyra Banks crashed the set of fellow diva Mariah Carey's Las Vegas show for the most iconic reunion, maybe ever. And, lucky for us, the whole thing was caught on camera. 

In a video shared to Klum's Instagram account, she and Banks strutted out on stage in matching black silk robes to join Carey for an impromptu glam session. Touching up the pop star's makeup mid-performance, Klum had a hairbrush in her hand and combed the singer's long, honey-blonde locks and Banks powdered her face.

Carey then took the brushes away from them and returned the favor, before blowing them each kisses as they exited the stage.

Courteney Cox shares swimsuit selfie with lookalike mum and daughter

Publish date: 2024-03-22

Courteney Cox shared a stunning swimsuit selfie celebrating three generations of beautiful women in her family. 

The Friends star, 56, hugged her mum, Courteney Copeland, 86, and daughter, Coco, 16 - who were also in swimwear - for the natural, beach photograph which had her Instagram followers gushing about how alike they looked.

The throwback snapshot was posted during the COVID-19 lockdown and gave a glimpse into the close family connection.

Courteney captioned the photo: "Here we are, my mom, Coco and me wrapped together in our generational trifecta. I am so grateful to be my daughter’s mother, and my mother’s daughter.

"This is just one of the many beautiful memories I will always treasure. Whether I’m right next to you in the ocean, or oceans apart, I love you always Moma."

Some of her followers got emotional over the post with one commenting: "I got choked up seeing this pic," and another writing: "You look just like your mum."

30 trendy lemonade tribal braids hairstyles for all seasons

Publish date: 2024-06-22

Are you looking for a new cute way to wear braids? Consider lemonade tribal braids. They have been trending since 2016 when Beyonce released the Lemonade album sporting the look. The braids have quickly gained a global appeal, and women of all ages are rocking the timeless and effortless look.

Lemonade braids are some of the most popular African braids hairstyles. The style is pretty easy to achieve as it requires you to plait side cornrows beginning at the front of your scalp. Then, you can gradually enlarge the cornrows as you move on. You can enhance the hairstyle by adding hair accessories to the braids.

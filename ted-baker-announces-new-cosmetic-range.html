Ted Baker announces first ever cosmetic collection 'With Ted' and we want it all

Publish date: 2024-04-24

TED Baker has announced its first ever cosmetics collection, 'With Ted' and we want it all.

The beauty line includes a range of eye, lip and cheek essentials, which will be available at ten locations across the UK as well as online.

The carefully curated range, has been made with innovative textures for easy, effortless application, exquisite shades to enhance and illuminate all skin tones, and has been finished in striking, compact packaging - we've fallen in love.

The cosmetic collection will launch on 31 January, and you can be the first to find out more by signing up here.

Beauty fans can find a number of lip products, including satin and matte finishes, glitzy glosses, creamy chubby lip pencils and intense statement lip colours.

The collection also includes luxury eye quads for accentuated eyes, a volumizing mascara, eyeliner market pen and metallic eye colours.

For a flawless complexion, there are blushers, bronzers, and exceptional highlighters pressed into luxurious textures.

The full collection is an 59 piece set, which will be available from ten in store locations across the UK, and will be available at tedbaker.com.

  • 61 Buchanan St, Glasgow

  • St Peters Arcade, Liverpool

  • 75 Brompton Road, Knightsbridge, London
  • 245 Regent Street, London

To celebrate the launch, Ted Baker is also setting up Ted's Townhouse in West London.

Beauty fans who live nearby can sign up for a free ticket to the Townhouse event on the 1 January at 12 Burleigh Street.

Shoppers will be able to exclusively shop the collection in store, as well as the chance to receive an express makeover from one of Ted's beauty champions, where you can choose a signature look and learn how to recreate it at home.

Tickets are available from Eventbrite for free here.

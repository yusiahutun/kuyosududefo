Joe Biden Is Signing Autographs and Taking Names

Publish date: 2024-05-31

Since his time in office, former Vice President Joe Biden has been busy starting the Biden foundation, officiating weddings and starring in our favorite #throwbackthursday photo. In November, a picture of a young (read: hot) Biden took over the Internet, and incited its own crop of memes.

The hype surrounding the photo just doubled. Yesterday, when the former VP gave a speech at George Mason University, two fans came equipped with a blown-up version of the viral picture—and Biden signed it.

According to Buzzfeed News, students Ravin Hassan and Milena Michael asked him to autograph the poster "that has been hanging in their kitchen since January." Now their beloved photo has a sweet handwritten note reading, "Keep the faith!" from Biden himself.

"He laughed so hard," the students spilled. "He told us that this [was taken] the weekend that he met his wife."

We just fell for him all over again.

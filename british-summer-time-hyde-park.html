British Summer Time at Hyde Park is back! See 2023's biggest acts

Publish date: 2024-02-16

This year London will be hosting Bruce Springsteen, Pink and Guns N' Roses – hurry, tickets are selling fast!

    Summer time in London is a wonderful thing and the British Summer Time music festival at Hyde Park - a series of open air concerts, right in the city centre - is top of our 'to do' list. The 2023 BST line up does not disappoint; expect to see Bruce Springsteen, Pink, Take That, Blackpink and Billy Joel live in concert. With some events already sold out, there are a few tickets left, but you will have to be quick to join in the fun!

    Kicking off on Saturday 24 June is P!nk, who brings her Summer Carnival to Hyde Park and returns the day afterwards on Sunday due to popular demand. 

    The other headliners of the series include big names such as Bruce Springsteen, Take That, Blackpink, Billy Joel and many more. And it's not just the headliners that make this festival stand out. Sugababes, The Script and Will Young will be joining the Take That men when they take to the stage on 1st July, and each day the support acts are worth turning up early to catch!

    MORE: Take That announce details of their new album during HELLO!'s Daily Lowdown podcast

    In addition to world-class music acts, it's a real party atmosphere in Hyde Park with the on-site food vendors offering a huge range of cuisines from Caribbean to Italian, burgers to waffles, vegan to pescatarian, a step above your typical 'festival food' vans. We would recommend turning up at opening doors to allow you and your friends and family time to enjoy the food, drink and electric atmosphere while the supporting acts build up into the evening! Tickets are available to buy for any one of the events, the only problem will be deciding who you want to see most!

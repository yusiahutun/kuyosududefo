The Cast of Netflix's 'Selling the OC' Brings the Drama in New Trailer

Publish date: 2024-02-08

Los Angeles's top real estate brokerage is heading to Orange County.

Selling the OC is the latest iteration of Netflix's mega-popular real estate reality drama Selling Sunset from reality television veteran Adam DiVello (Laguna Beach, The Hills, The City). The new series follows real estate tycoons Jason and Brett Oppenheim as they launch a new agency in the heart of Orange County, home to some of Southern California's most spectacular properties.

Similar to Selling Sunset, the OC cast is a delightfully catty—and competitive—bunch who are sure to bring the drama come the series premiere on August 24. Unlike with the Los Angeles–based crew, however, the OC offshoot is a coed affair. Though seven superstar female agents make up the boutique agency, four male realtors are in on the action this time, adding a new level of drama to the binge-worthy series. (See an exclusive first look at the full cast in the images below.)

"It's fun to see a cast with both males and females," DiVello tells BAZAAR.com of the new cast, hinting at the drama ahead. "Things can get complicated when spending so much time together in and outside the office."

From left to right: Gio Helou, Brett Oppenheim, Tyler Stanaland, Jason Oppenheim, Sean Palmieri, and Austin Victoria in Selling the OC.

"Fans will get an inside look at the ultra-luxurious coastal homes, beaches, and lifestyle of Orange County, California. Unlike Selling Sunset with its established group of agents with long-standing relationships, Jason has opened a brand-new office with a new team of realtors who are getting to know one another," DiVello continues. "They not only sell amazing properties, but don't hold back when it comes to revealing their personal lives or their opinions of each other."

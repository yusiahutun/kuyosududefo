Full Moon in Virgo Will Affect Each Zodiac Sign 2024

Publish date: 2024-04-24

An intriguing full moon is underway, signaling a period of discernment and meticulous attention to detail. Whether it’s decluttering your living space or enhancing your productivity levels, you’ll be as inspired as ever to learn more about how the full moon in Virgo will affect each zodiac sign. In the meantime, feel free to grab your cleaning gloves, as this is an opportunity to bring order and clarity into your world. By no means is this an overnight overhaul; on the contrary, even small steps towards organizing and refining different aspects of your life can bring you a sense of accomplishment.

If you’ve ever wondered why your Virgo peers and loved ones constantly look for ways to improve themselves and the world around them—removing what isn’t healthy, productive, or necessary to create a more efficient lifestyle—here’s why. Governed byMercury—planet of communication, thought processes, and our immediate surroundings—the essence of Virgo has everything to do with intellect, precision, and, above all, purification. This mutable earth sign is often associated with discernment and structure, not just in the physical dimension, but when in regards to one’s mental and emotional well being. Where Mercury is symbolic of insight and hard data as Gemini’s planetary ruler, it represents the (communication) synergy that occurs between the mind, body, and soul, when in the realm of Virgo. 

fThis is all the more significant to consider during the upcoming full moon on February 24. Happening via 5 degrees of this mutable earth sign at 7:30 a.m. ET, it’s important to reference back to the new moon in Virgo that took place back in September 2023, as your seeds of intention are likely starting to come to fruition. For instance, the full moon phase marks the midpoint of the lunar cycle. This brings a sense of culmination and completion, as it illuminates areas of our lives that may have been overlooked in the past or perhaps where our emotions need to be integrated.

A number of astrological aspects influence the energy that is emanating from this lunation. The full moon’s ruler (Mercury) will be transiting elusive Pisces, a zodiac sign where the messenger planet is believed to be in its detriment. This is because of Mercury’s keen attention to detail and innate desire to collect information and relay the message. But in nebulous Pisces, the messenger planet finds itself in a realm where intuition and emotion reign over the facts. This can, in turn, lead to a mindset and communication style that is more ambiguous than logical. That said, with Mercury (the full moon’s ruler) leaving us with no choice but to go inward, there is still something intriguing to explore and discover along the way. 

Mercury will, after all, be in close proximity to the sun andSaturn in Pisces, which can also shed light upon something we aren’t yet aware of and need to see. This could also come in the form of a reality check or an uncomfortable conversation. Unconscious boundaries and limitations could also be made apparent under this lunation, specifically, those that contribute to the dynamics and productivity levels within your day-to-day life. On a brighter note, the full moon will form a harmonious trine to Jupiter— planet of abundance, expansion, and spiritual wisdom—adding an auspicious layer of stability and patience.

This means, if there’s something you’re continuously contemplating or investing energy into that isn’t in alignment with your core values, this is also being brought to your attention at this time. Jupiter will also be at odds with Mars and Venus in Aquarius, which continues to illuminate everything from your individual freedom to your future goals, but what’s no longer resonating with your sense of security or the person you’re becoming?

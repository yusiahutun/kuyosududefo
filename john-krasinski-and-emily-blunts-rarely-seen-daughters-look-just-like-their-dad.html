John Krasinski and Emily Blunt's rarely-seen daughters look just like their dad

Publish date: 2024-02-17

John Krasinski and Emily Blunt took a trip to the US Open semi-finals with their ‘rarely-seen’ daughters, and they look just like their dad! The family watched Novak Djokovic beat Ben Shelton on the way to winning his record-breaking 24th Grand Slam Title.

Many celebrities and their families, including children Hazel, 9, and Violet, 7, sat in the stands of the New York Grand Slam. Kylie Jenner and Timothee Chalamet also made headlines as they confirmed their relationship at the event with a lot of PDA.

Emily Blunt and John Krasinski’s daughters seen in the stands

In a rare public family outing, the foursome looked chic and casual as they sat in the stands of the match. Youngest daughter Violet seemed to match her mom as they both sported cute floral dresses.

Hazel and her father opted for a more sporty look, with John wearing a navy blue polo to match the court, as Hazel rocked a Boston Red Sox hat.

The two seemed engrossed in the play as they sipped drinks throughout the match. Could we have the next Venus and Serena on our hands?

The Hollywood couple wed in July 2010, four years before welcoming eldest, Hazel, following Violet’s birth two years later.

Although the family is based in the US, in 2016, Blunt revealed her daughter actually spoke with a British accent like herself at first. However, the American accent grew over time.

Speaking to Jimmy Fallon at the time, the movie star revealed: “Hazel had my accent for a long time and would say things like ‘water’ which I was really proud of.”

“And then the other day she goes, American accent] ‘Can I have some water?’ And I went, ‘It’s [English accent] water.’ And she was like, ‘No, it’s [American accent] water.‘”

However, both Violet and Hazel attended a school in London over the pandemic, both once again picking up the British accent, as Blunt joked she was ‘delighted’.

Emily Blunt ‘takes a break’ from acting after Oppenheimer release

After her stunning blockbuster performance alongside Cillian Murphy in the Christopher Nolan film, Oppenheimer, the 40-year-old announced she would be taking a break from acting.

In a sit-down chat with the Table for Two podcast, The Devil Wears Prada star revealed she wasn’t taking on any more work this year, to focus on motherhood.

“This year, I’m not working. I worked quite a bit last year and my oldest baby is nine, so we’re in the last year of single digits,” she revealed.

I just feel [like] there are cornerstones to their day that are so important when they’re little,” she added.

However, fear not, as fans can still see Blunt this year in the new Netflix crime drama, Pain in the Hustlers.

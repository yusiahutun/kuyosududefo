What is a Fragrance-free Shampoo?

A fragrance-free shampoo is the one that is infused with coconut-based or plant-derived cleansing agents. It doesn't cause itchiness, redness, allergies, and inflammation, rather it cleanses your scalp, nourishes your tresses, and quenches the thirst of your parched, colored, or chemically-treated tresses. Addedly, it is completely free from harsh ingredients and synthetic fragrances that never hamper your hair and scalp health. To note more, for anyone who is dealing with sensitive skin or scalp, a fragrance-free shampoo is the go-to!

    How to Choose the Best Fragrance-free Shampoos?

    Here are a few aspects that you should consider before splurging on the best fragrance-free shampoo -

    Hair Type: Make sure you analyze your hair type before investing in any of the fragrance-free shampoos. For those possessing sensitive, irritated scalp and thin hair, investing in a shampoo that features a gentle and mild formula is deemed as a wise decision. Likewise, for those with oily scalps, relying on a clarifying shampoo can be beneficial. 

    Ingredients: Always pay heed to the list of ingredients. It is wise to invest in an unscented shampoo that is enriched with natural ingredients, essential nutrients, botanical extracts, vitamins, proteins, antioxidants, and coconut-based cleansing agents. Avoid purchasing shampoos that contain artificial fragrances, harsh chemicals, synthetic ingredients, toxins, parabens, silicones, alcohol, and sulfates to avoid any allergic reaction.

    Formula: The next important aspect is the formulation of the shampoo. It is advisable to splurge on a non-comedogenic, vegan, cruelty-free, and hypoallergenic shampoo to keep scalp irritation, redness, and itchiness at bay.  

    Texture: The texture and consistency of the shampoo should neither be too thick nor too runny. It is advisable to choose a shampoo that has a medium gel-like or creamy texture that lathers well. Stay away from shampoos with very thick consistency as they might leave a residue behind or demand rigorous rinsing and cleansing.

    FAQ's

    Is scent-free the same as fragrance-free?

    Not really. Scent-free shampoos may contain certain ingredients to mask the smell of the shampoo, whereas a fragrance-free shampoo may be completely free from artificial and natural fragrances.

    Does fragrance in shampoo damage hair?

    Yes, a fragranced shampoo can damage your hair and lead to hair fall.

    Do fragrance-free shampoos contain harmful chemicals?

    No, fragrance-free shampoos don’t contain any harmful chemicals; rather they are formulated with clean, pure, natural, and gentle cleansing agents.

    Do fragrance-free shampoos work well for all hair types?

    Yes, a few of the fragrance-free shampoos work well for all hair types, whereas some are specially crafted for oily, dry, damaged, and sensitive scalp.

    Why choose a fragrance-free shampoo?

    One should use a fragrance-free shampoo to prevent scalp irritation, redness, and allergies.

    Are fragrance-free shampoos better for your hair?

    Yes, fragrance-free shampoos are better for your hair as they cause less hair damage and breakage, and help in preventing dandruff.

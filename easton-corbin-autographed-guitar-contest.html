Win an Autographed Acoustic Guitar From Easton Corbin

Publish date: 2024-05-25

Easton Corbin has just released one of the most anticipated sophomore albums in recent years. 'All Over the Road,' which hit stores and digital retailers on Sept. 18, features the hit single 'Lovin' You Is Fun' and 10 other cuts sure to please fans of more traditional country music. To commemorate the new album, the singer wants to give you an autographed acoustic guitar plus a handwritten note!

Enter by submitting your email address below. Only one entry per person is allowed, and be sure to read the official Taste of Country contest rules before entering. One winner will be chosen after the contest ends on Oct. 11, 2012. The guitar you can win by signing up below is a Mitchell MD-100.

Corbin's self-titled first album was one of the more refreshing debuts in recent history, and the new album looks to follow up on the sound he brought to hits like 'Roll With It' and 'A Little More Country Than That.' Buy 'All Over the Road' here.

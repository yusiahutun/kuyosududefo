Dionysian Productions Shutting Down

Publish date: 2024-09-11

BURLINGTON, VT (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — The management company for Phish, Dionysian Productions, will close it’s Burlington office at the end of the year.

“It’s time for me to wipe the slate clean,” owner John Paluska told CelebrityAccess. “I’ve been managing Phish since I was 21 years old; my whole adult life. I felt now was a good juncture to make a clean break from managing and music and see what else is out there.”

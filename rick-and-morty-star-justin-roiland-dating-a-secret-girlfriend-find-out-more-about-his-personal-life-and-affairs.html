Justin Roiland's Career

Justin moved to Los Angeles in 2004 and acted in numerous short movies initially. He then featured regularly on Comedy Central's The Sarah Silverman Program as 'Blonde Craig'.

Roiland now works as the voice actor in Rick and Morty for both the lead characters, where he is also the co-creator, co-writer, and executive producer along with Dan Harmon.

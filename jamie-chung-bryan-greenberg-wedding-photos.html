Jamie Chung &amp; Bryan Greenberg Are Married -- See the First Official Wedding Photo!

Publish date: 2024-08-22

Jamie Chung and Bryan Greenberg are officially off the market!

The "Once Upon a Time" star married the actor at the El Capitan Canyon Resort in Santa Barbara, Calif. on Saturday.

The couple's rustic-chic celebration, which will be featured in an upcoming issue of Martha Stewart Weddings, started on Friday with a "Boos and Booze" costume dinner party. The pair said "I Do" the following day in a hilltop ceremony in front of more than 200 guests.

The beautiful bride looked stunning in a long-sleeved lace Monique Lhuillier gown while her new hubby looked dapper in a blue suit. The "Real World" alum and fashion blogger dressed her bridesmaids in pale blue, grey and black dresses from Lovers + Friends.

Instagram

Before the pair got hitched, the groom shared a photo of his Marty McFly costume for Friday's pre-wedding, Halloween-themed festivities. "The future's almost here!" he cleverly captioned the Instagram shot.

Jamie and Bryan were first linked since 2012 and got engaged over the holidays in 2014, which they celebrated with a romantic trip to Turks and Caicos. The actress and her beau starred together in "A Year and Change" earlier this year and are currently working on the movie, "Already Tomorrow," in Hong Kong.

Congrats to the happy couple!

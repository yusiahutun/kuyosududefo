Transitioning to female

Lia started her transition in May 2019 with the help of hormone replacement therapy. During her junior year, she revealed to her coaches, friends and teammates that she was transgender. At the time she was undergoing hormone therapy, she was required to swim for the men's team.

When Thomas was competing in the men's team, she was ranked 554 in the 200 freestyle, 65 in the 500 freestyle, and 32 in the 1650 freestyle. However, that has changed in the women's team because she currently ranks 5 in the 200 freestyle, first in the 500 freestyle, and eighth in the 1650 freestyle.

The controversial participation of Lia in the women's team evoked public debate in the United States.

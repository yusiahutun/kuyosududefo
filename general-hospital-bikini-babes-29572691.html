US soap opera General Hospital has birthed a wealth of stars over the years, including future Suits actress and Duchess of Sussex Meghan Markle – and plenty wow off-camera too

General Hospital has birthed a huge number of stars over the years, plenty of whom went on to have huge careers in Hollywood.

From future Suits star and Duchess of Sussex Meghan Markle to Hollywood icon Eva Longoria, plenty of huge actresses have appeared on the daytime soap opera.

And it’s not just while the cameras are rolling that the stars know how to wow their audiences.

Off-camera, plenty have stripped down for sultry bikini and swimwear shoots, proving themselves to be bombshells in their own right.

General Hospital alum Kristina Wagner stripped down for her first ever bikini snaps aged 54, and looked absolutely incredible while doing so.

Back in 2017, the actress made her bikini cover debut for First For Women magazine, slipping into a tiny red two-piece surrounded by lush greenery at the side of an outdoor pool.

The actress confessed: “Never in my life have I posed in a bikini or underwear before, but I didn’t mind because it’s not about, ‘How do you get fit so you can wear a bikini?’. It’s about lifestyle and having a good body image.”

And over on Meghan Markle’s old Instagram account, the 90210 beauty posed up a storm while doing some seaside yoga in nothing but a tiny khaki two-piece.

Meghan raised one leg high in the air as she formed her body into a crab position – looking totally unlike her royal persona.

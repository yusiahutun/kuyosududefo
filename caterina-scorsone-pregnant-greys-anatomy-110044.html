CONGRATS!Greys Anatomy Star Caterina Scorsone is Pregnant With Baby No. 2

Publish date: 2024-07-30

Congratulations, Caterina Scorsone!

The 34-year-old Grey’s Anatomy star is pregnant with her second baby, her rep confirmed to People earlier this week.

Caterina on Aug. 4.

Salma Hayek Kicks Off Bikini Season in the Most Salma Hayek Way Possible

Publish date: 2024-04-02

Salma Hayek is getting an early start to her summer.

The Frida star graced us all with a fresh crop of bikini photos, which she generously shared with her over 28 million followers on Instagram this past week.

In the new photo dump, Hayek wears a patterned dark blue triangle bikini and a stack of gold necklaces while hanging off of a wooden and chrome ladder off the edge of a boat. In a few frames, she lifts her hands to shield her disgruntled face as someone off-camera appears to shoot water at her. “When your family won’t let you take a bikini ріс in peace 🥲🚿🤣,” she cheekily captions the pictures.

Hayek is known for her love of documenting her swimsuit-clad adventures on social media. In another post from earlier this month, she takes a selfie while decked out in a low-cut lapis one-piece, plus huge reflective blue shades and a silver medallion necklace. She captions the post simply, “It’s okay to be blue 💙 #azul.”

The actor talked about her love of dropping frequent bikini photo dumps in an interview with Entertainment Tonight in February 2021, describing the act as “liberating.”

“I had to lose a lot of weight and exercise to get into the bikini towards the end of last year. I’m glad I took a lot of pictures, I have no shame on it, because it was the first week of the vacation,” she told the outlet. “But after that first week and when I heard that everybody was going back into quarantine, I started eating again.”

Early Life And Education Of Coyote Peterson

Coyote Peterson was born on September 1, 1981, in Newbury, Ohio, USA. The wildlife explorer grew up in the small town of Newbury, Ohio. He graduated from Ohio State University.

His interest in the wildlife came when he once had a battle with a snapping turtle. He nicknamed the turtle, "The Dragon." 

Peterson was curious about wildlife and wild creatures from the very young age which became his profession later on.

Coyote Peterson's Career

Peterson began his career working on movie projects along with his friends. Later, he starred in the animal show on Discovery such as  Breaking Trail, Dragon Trial, and Coyote’s Backyard. His work on Trial series won him an Emmy Award.

Afterwards, Coyote made his appearance in several shows and interviews. His show Brave Wilderness and Animalist is featured in YouTube channel and has got billions of views. He has posted numerous videos on his channel including Pinched by a Huge Crab, Stung by a Bullet Ant and Stung by a Cow Killer. During his career journey, he has worked alongside Bear Grylls who is also an outdoor host and an animal lover.

He has also published the book Coyote Peterson’s Brave Adventures: Wild Animals in Wild World featuring his adventures around the world. The book was Amazon’s #1 Bestselling book and the stories are based on children’s interest.

What is hotel heiress Nicky Hilton Rothschild up to in 2024? Paris Hiltons younger sister shares

Publish date: 2024-06-13
Despite their shared fame, Nicky does not share Paris’s enthusiasm for celebrity status. She famously turned down an opportunity to appear alongside Paris on Fox’s hit reality show The Simple Life in 2003. The show’s 20th anniversary revealed Nicky’s deliberate move away from reality television – a path that led Nicole Richie to co-star with Paris instead.

Curious about what Nicky Hilton Rothschild is up to now? Here’s an update on her life nowadays:

How’s the relationship between sisters Nicky and Paris Hilton?

Nicky and Paris, 43, have always shared a close bond. However in the past few years that bond has become even stronger. Speaking to People, the 40-year-old entrepreneur shared how her relationship with her sister has evolved, especially now that they are both mothers.

“Normally, around this season, we’re brainstorming New Year’s Eve plans, contemplating a Vegas trip. However, this year, our discussions have shifted towards planning a trip involving all our children,” Nicky told the magazine.

Has fashion always been Nicky’s biggest passion?

Her earliest fashion venture, Chick by Nicky Hilton in 2004, later became the upscale Nicky Hilton Beverly Hills in 2006. Later, her foray into high fashion with Nicholai by Nicky Hilton in 2011 debuted at Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week in New York.

Her partnerships and collaborations, including handbag designs for Samantha Thavasa, a line with Linea Pelle, and the Mommy and Me collection with Tolani – all showcase her distinctive style. Nowadays her focus is on shoes, actively collaborating with flats brand French Sole.

Nicky Hilton and James Rothschild’s fairy tale wedding

Nicky and James Rothschild, the sole heir of British banker Amschel Rothschild and a member of the renowned Rothschild and Guinness clans, met at a mutual friend’s wedding in Rome in 2011. This meeting led to their 2014 engagement and a lavish 2015 London wedding at Kensington Palace’s Orangery, where Nicky donned a US$77,000 Valentino gown.

How’s Nicky Hilton Rothschild’s life with her family of four?

Now the couple enjoys family life on a large Hamptons property with their three young children: six-year-old Lily-Grace Victoria, Theodora “Teddy” Marilyn, age five, and one-year-old Chasen. Nicky remains open to expanding their family, saying to E! News, “You know, never say never. But we’re very happy right now.”

‘We’re done’: Eric Decker and Jessie James Decker just had their last kid

The couple welcomed their son in June 2022 but chose to keep his name private. Only this past March, nearly two years later, Nicky revealed her son’s name: Chasen. Speaking to Us Weekly, the 40-year-old socialite and mother of three shared her affection for the distinctive name but refrained from discussing why the announcement was delayed.

Is Nicky Hilton Rothschild really committed to sustainability?

In 2021, she introduced her first 100 per cent sustainable line with French Sole, marking a major step in environmental dedication.

Speaking to The Daily Front Row, Nicky detailed how she integrates eco-friendly practices into daily life by using recyclable bags, saving energy and choosing sustainable goods: “I’m not perfect, but I try and do my part.”

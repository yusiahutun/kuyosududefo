Minka Kelly Voted Sexiest Woman Alive!

Publish date: 2024-06-11

"Friday Night Lights" star Minka Kelly was just voted Sexiest Woman Alive by Esquire Magazine -- and, at 30, she's never felt better about her body!

Of turning the big 3-0, Derek Jeter's girlfriend and daughter of Aerosmith guitarist Rick Dufay tells the mag "I love it. I think it's the most exciting birthday I think I've ever had. I'm so excited to be a woman. Done with the twenties. I'm confident — confident in my skin, and I'm cool with my flaws and all that stuff."

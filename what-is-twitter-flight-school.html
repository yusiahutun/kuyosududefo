What Is Twitter Flight School?

Publish date: 2024-02-13

Twitter Flight School enables you to learn social media branding and marketing from experts to stay relevant in an ever-changing global job market. You can effortlessly build on your Twitter know-how and develop social media marketing skills thanks to Twitter Flight School.

Here, we'll take a look at the Twitter Flight School fundamentals and let you take an informed decision before investing precious time in its courses.

What Is Twitter Flight School?

Twitter Flight School is an online learning program from Twitter. It offers high-quality courses that help you learn brand promotion, advertising, and social media marketing on Twitter.

Twitter Flight School has learning content for everyone, irrespective of your status—you can be an experienced professional or a fresher seeking a career in social media marketing on Twitter.

You can pick the course tailored for you by choosing a course of any level, that too with no complicated exams or time constraints. Twitter thoughtfully created the pace of the courses, keeping working professionals in mind.

You don’t have to put a strain on your professional or personal life while learning from Twitter Flight School. Nor does it try to replace all of your calendar schedules or commitments.

The courses and their content are modular. Therefore, you can choose tiny and bite-sized learning content, depending on your daily study goals.

The learning content of Twitter Flight School is highly flexible. You can simply go through the video campaigns if you hold experience in social media marketing and are only interested in the know-hows of Twitter advertising.

On the other hand, if you have set up goals to learn social media marketing and brand promotion on Twitter from the start, you can take up the milestone-based coursework.

Twitter Flight School Course Content

It’s not some boring learning website that provides you with mundane text-based courses. Twitter Flight School offers you a perfect blend of different learning content in modular packages.

The learning elements keep changing to keep you engaged. It has the following course varieties:

  • Downloadable resources that help you in your professional career.
  • Truly interactive coursework for engaged sessions.
  • Success stories that inspire you.
  • Personalized learning pathways for self-recognition.

    • What You Can Expect from Twitter Flight School

    Twitter Flight School is more of a vast knowledge library on social media advertising and brand promotion. You can expect lessons and how-tos from this platform that will ultimately meet your requirements.

    The followings are the features that you can expect:

  • Learning content that helps you with creative ideation.
  • How-to courses that assist you in planning branding and marketing strategies.
  • Get inspirations and suggestions to create your own teaching content.
  • Tailored coursework just for you.
  • Learning content that updates automatically.
  • Get recommendations on which you can act upon.
  • A badge that you can add to your portfolio.

    • Who Can Get Admitted to Twitter Flight School?

    Anyone passionate about branding and marketing on social media should sign up for Twitter Flight School. Its modular coursework doesn’t make it mandatory to go through the whole course to extract specific knowledge

    So, from experts to novices, this platform is for everyone and everything about marketing and branding on Twitter.

    The followings are the notable roles that can enjoy several benefits from this learning portal:

  • Social media marketing managers or associates of any brand or agency, those want to create a strategy and execute it with efficiency.
  • If you’re a Twitter partner who needs to brush up on their knowledge of online branding and marketing.
  • It’s an ideal platform for freelance social media marketing professionals to learn new strategies cost-effectively.
  • An owner of a small business with a budget constraint can efficiently utilize this platform to be their own social media marketing expert.

    • Why Is Twitter Flight School Important?

    Primarily, social media marketing enthusiasts should choose this platform for easy-to-understand coursework and interactive learning. However, there are many other vital reasons for you to choose Twitter Flight School, and these are:

  • A truly free learning portal.
  • Access the latest tips and tricks of social media marketing from one of the leaders of social media, Twitter.
  • A clean and fresh-looking learning interface that offers separate sections for learning pathway tracking (History) and storing favorite courses (Favorites).
  • It shows you automatic notifications to continue a paused course or start one of your favorite courses.
  • You can choose learning pathways or individual content based on your current role.
  • Advance courses let you become job-ready and acquire more clients to whom you can provide social media marketing consultations.
  • The platform helps you to stand out from the crowd through verifiable badges.

    • How to Choose Coursework on Twitter Flight School

    The Topics section helps you to choose appropriate coursework depending on your learning objectives. You’ll find this section in the top-right corner near the Search box.

    Once you expand the section, you’ll see a sidebar with options such as Earn a Badge, Twitter Ad Fundamentals, #FirstLooks, and five more.

    Let’s say that you’re a freelance social media marketer, and you want to earn badges that you can add to your digital portfolio. Here is how you might want to proceed:

  • Select the Earn a Badge option from the Topics sidebar.
  • The portal will show you the course pathways in this section. For example, the Performance Fundamentals Badge.
  • Click on it to view the complete coursework outline and the modules.
  • Complete the modules as they appear to earn a badge.

    • Badges You Can Earn on Twitter Flight School

    Badges are the awards that prove that you have mastered a specific level of skill by taking up courses from Twitter Flight School.

    You can showcase the badge on your blog, website, LinkedIn, or other social media platform to attract clients or employers. It’s a perfect collectible that increases the value of your freelancing portfolio.

    The badge shows how much time you have invested in a course and the validity of the reward. You can earn as many badges as you want by participating in instructor-led or self-paced courses. However, you need to pass the course assessments too.

    Twitter Flight School offers the following three badges: Launch and Connect, Performance Fundamentals, and Twitter Video. Twitter keeps updating its coursework and badge earning requirements. Your badge may expire if the coursework has been updated substantially.

    You can always re-take the updated course to earn the badge again. There is no constraint on attempts for earning a badge. You can go through the course and assessments multiple times to earn a badge.

    Online Courses to Help Grow Your Career

    You don’t need to visit any institute to learn new skills. Learning resources, training, and certifications are at your fingertips, thanks to the internet. And, learning skills from social media leaders like Twitter can add value to your professional portfolio.

    Moreover, you can become a mentor for others by utilizing platforms that enable you to create courses online.

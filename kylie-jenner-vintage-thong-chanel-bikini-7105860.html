Kylie Jenner's Vintage Thong Bikini Is Older Than She Is

Publish date: 2024-10-08

Kylie Jenner and an itty-bitty bikini always make a great pair. But when that bikini is vintage Chanel and covered in dozens of Swarovski crystals? It's guaranteed to be a match made in fashion heaven. 

On Saturday, Kylie shared her latest OOTD from her Caribbean getaway in the Turks and Caicos, and it included a bejeweled tiny two-piece with thong bottoms and mismatched neon color blocks. In a slideshow captioned "daydream," the beauty mogul modeled the bathing suit — which consisted of a spliced pink and blue triangle top with rows upon rows of rhinestones and high-cut lime green cheeky briefs that featured an interlocking CC logo from behind. According to Cosmopolitan, Kylie's vintage Chanel bikini first made its debut on the spring 1995 runway (making it older than she is) and was designed by Karl Lagerfeld. 

She wore her long dark hair down in natural waves, and paired her sunburnt cheeks with dewy skin and minimal makeup. Accessory-wise, Kylie seemingly used a braided lime green and yellow puffy purse as her beach bag.  

Earlier this week, Kylie opted for a less-conventional bikini look while wearing head-to-toe Jacquemus. Pairing a black bandeau top underneath a sheer long-sleeved shirt with a crisscrossing cord across her stomach, Jenner wore a green printed miniskirt with matching thong bottoms peeking out from the waistband. She finished off her vacation-ready outfit with an acid yellow faux-fur bag and strappy sandals adorned with feathers.

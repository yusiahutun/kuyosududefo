Jennifer Lopez Catfishes Us All With Versace Boots That Look Like Jeans

Publish date: 2024-08-08

Hello, yes, 911? I’d like to report a case of stolen identity. Jennifer Lopez’s Versace boots appear to be impersonating a pair of miniature jeans and I’m concerned. They even have their own leather belt and tiny pockets, please hurry.

The singer stepped out in the denim Versace boots yesterday, making us all do a double-take: are they jeans or are they boots? This is a case for the FBI.

Gotham//Getty Images

Embracing the perplexing fashion hybrid, JLo skipped wearing pants altogether and styled the over-the-knee denim boots with an oversized white button-down shirt. Despite the fact that the boots are going through a severe identity crisis, she managed to make it a full LOOK anyway.

Gotham//Getty Images

Versace then shared a close-up of the jean boots and revealed that in addition to a leather belt, they also have tiny pockets (which seems fully necessary).

Now the real question is: what is JLo carrying around in her boot pockets?

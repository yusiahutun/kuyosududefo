Ron DeSantis Enlists Billy Dean For Presidential Campaign Theme Song: Never Back Down

Publish date: 2024-09-30

Ron DeSantis and Billy Dean

NASHVILLE (CelebrityAccess) – Florida governor Ron DeSantis has enlisted the voice of Grammy Award-winning country artist Billy Dean for his official 2024 presidential campaign theme song. The tune, “Never Back Down,” debuted Wednesday (January 10) in a campaign promo video posted to DeSantis’ YouTube channel. DeSantis plans to play the song at all upcoming campaign events. Watch and/or listen HERE.

“I’m a Florida native and resident who has lived the American Dream,” explains Dean. “My dad served in World War II and lost two of his brothers to the cause. They sacrificed their dreams so I could live mine. My dad did not want me to serve the country with guns and bullets, but I could serve with my pen, paper, and guitar. Ron DeSantis is not just Florida’s governor; he is America’s governor, and he inspired me to write and record a song as a message to all those who put power and party above country. You won’t get away with it without a fight. I owe it to our veterans to ‘Never Back Down!'”

Dean co-wrote the song with fellow Floridians Hugh and Cody VanLandingham, and it will be available for streaming and download this Friday (January 12).

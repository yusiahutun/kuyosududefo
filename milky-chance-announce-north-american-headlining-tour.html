Milky Chance Announce North American Headlining Tour

Publish date: 2024-07-19

Milky Chance. Credit: Anthony Molina.

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – Milky Chance — fresh off the release of their critically acclaimed new album Mind The Moon — have today (Dec. 9) announced an extensive North American tour.

Slated to kick off on March 23 in Dallas, TX, the 20+ date trek will make stops in Portland, Nashville, Seattle, Atlanta, and more, before wrapping up in Los Angeles on May 20.

Tickets go on sale to the general public on Friday, December 13 @ 10:00 am CST via Ticketmaster.com.

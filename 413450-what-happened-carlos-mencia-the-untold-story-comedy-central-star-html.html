In comedy, originality is sacred. While comedians such as Dave Chappell have confessed giving some of their jokes to their peers, the cases are rare. However, Carlos Mencia is one of the mainstream comedians to have many plagiarism accusations in Hollywood.

In his final season at Mind of Mencia, rumours about him stealing other people’s jokes started floating, especially in the entertainment circles. Although Comedy Central did not release any information on why the show was cancelled, many commentators believe plagiarism was one of the key reasons.

Shortly after exiting the network, Joe Rogan interrupted him in the middle of a show at Comedy Store on Sunset Boulevard. According to Rogan, Mencia was stealing his jokes without crediting him. Although Rogan no longer holds a grudge against him, the allegations affected his standup comedy career.

Sofia Coppola collaborates with Chanel on a film series to celebrate the latest Metiers d'Art coll

Publish date: 2024-07-28

Remember dinner parties? And dancing?

Sofia Coppola has partnered with Chanel to shoot a series of films celebrating the fashion house's latest Métiers d'art collection - the first of which you can watch exclusively, here.

The 2019/20 Métiers d'art show - Chanel's annual celebration of craftsmanship - took place in December in Paris, in the form of a catwalk event at the Grand Palais, followed by dinner and dancing at famed Paris nightlife haunt, La Coupole - a favourite of Karl Lagerfeld's. At the star-studded after-party, the models - including Gigi Hadid, Vittoria Ceretti and Mona Tougaard - celebrated with the front row guests - Kristen Stewart, Lily Rose Depp, Margaret Qualley and more - and enjoyed a live performance by Belgian singer Angèle, all decked out in glittering pieces from the show. The night's celebrations were all intimately captured by filmmakers Roman and Sofia Coppola (watch the video above).

Courtesy

The 2019/20 Métiers d'art collection - the first from creative director Virginie Viard - paid tribute to Chanel's historic address, 31 rue Cambon. For the show's setting, the brand reconstructed Mademoiselle Chanel’s apartment, the haute couture salons and the iconic mirrored staircase of the address, as devised by Viard in partnership with Coppola.

"We thought about the original shows that were held at 31 rue Cambon, and how great it must have been to see the models walking by so close, and the setting by Coco’s mirrored staircase, which always gives me a thrill to see,” explained Coppola.

Courtesy

To celebrate the collection landing in boutiques, Coppola also created a film that looks back over a century of creation at the heart of the famed address, which was acquired in 1918 by Gabrielle Chanel. The designer installed her studio, atelier and apartment at the address, which still remains there today.

Courtesy

For the final instalment in the video series, Roman and Sofia Coppola created a behind-the-scenes film focusing on one of the hero pieces from the new collection; the elegant two-tone pumps. They filmed the models dancing backstage at the show and making their way to La Coupole, to create a sweet fashion film focusing on feet.

The Paris – 31 rue Cambon 2019/20 Métiers d’art collection is now available in Chanel boutiques.

Jeremy Renner's ex Sonni Pacheco shows off blonde locks amid divorce settlement

Publish date: 2024-07-20

She just recently finalized her divorce with Avengers star Jeremy Renner.

And it looks as if Sonni Pacheco is ready for a new look as she moves forward following the split and settlement.

The 23-year-old showed off newly dyed blonde tresses as she emerged from Meche Salon in Beverly Hills on Sunday.

She also donned a black, zip-up jacket and a pair of black and white Nike sneakers for the salon visit, and sported a backpack slung over her shoulder.

Her newly dyed tresses were worn in a center part, and styled straight, now also shorter as they fell to just about shoulder length.

Laid-back: Sonni dressed comfortably for the long visit at Meche Salon, sporting a loose-fitting T-shirt, skintight leggings, and Nike sneakers

The big change comes after Sonni's ex-husband Jeremy has been ordered to pay her $13,000 in child support every month, following the finalization of their divorce, as reported by TMZ.

The sizable sum is to be paid by The Hurt Locker star to assist Sonni in looking after their two-year-old daughter Ava.

He will also have to pay Sonni five per cent of any excess when he makes more than than $2.3million in a year as additional child support.

Onward: Sonni and Jeremy's divorce was finalized at the end of December. The couple had gotten married in January 2014, almost 10 months after their child was born, and she filed to end their union less than a year later

However, Jeremy, who is worth an estimated $35million, will not have to pay spousal support due to their pre-nuptial agreement. The couple will also share custody of Ava. 

When Sonni filed for divorce earlier last year, she had sought spousal support and custody of their daughter.

Emma Stone Is Gorgeous in an Intricately Woven Pink Dress and Chrome Stilettos

Publish date: 2024-07-31

Emma Stone attended the 76th Directors Guild of America Awards yesterday looking pretty in a playful pink dress.

The La La Land star walked the red carpet at the Beverly Hilton in Los Angeles in a romantic piece pulled from the Givenchy Spring 2003 couture collection. The dress featured a wide, plunging neckline, two thick straps, and a form-fitting bodice that was constructed from tightly, intricately woven ribbons of fabric that separated at the hips to create a see-through, free-flowing, floor-grazing skirt. The streaming pieces danced as she walked to reveal a pair of silver chrome square-toe stilettos with a thin ankle strap. Stone kept her jewelry simple and minimal, opting for diamond stud earrings and a couple dainty rings. For the final touches, she accented the ensemble with soft, shimmery baby-pink eye shadow that made her green eyes pop; glowy, rosy blush; and a glossy strawberry pink lip. She wore her caramel-highlighted brown hair down and parted to the side.

Steve Granitz

Amy Sussman

Once inside the venue, the actor-producer added an oversize black blazer. Stone, alongside costar Mark Ruffalo, spoke onstage and presented director Yorgos Lanthimos with the DGA Feature Film Medallion for his 2023 film Poor Things, in which she plays the lead role, Bella Baxter.

Stone has been showing out in gorgeous ensembles all award season long. Last month at the Golden Globes, she put her bejeweled take on the naked dress trend on display in a custom-made Louis Vuitton gown. The sheer piece featured a plunging V-neck and high center slit and was covered in glittery champagne-colored sequins and an array of dainty pale pink and dusty-blue flower illustrations. She paired it all with strappy nude heels, double drop diamond earrings, and a yellow gemstone ring. That night, she took home Best Performance by a Female Actor in a Motion Picture – Musical or Comedy for her performance in Poor Things.

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce themed wrapping paper is perfect for Valentine's Day

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Valentine’s Day is just around the corner, and one Swiftie has come up with a wrapping paper that is enough to make gifts extra special for all Taylor Swift fans.

For the Swiftie nation, Taylor Swift has always been the unofficial mascot of Valentine’s Day. The 34-year-old celebrity’s documentation of her love life through her roaring twenties and early thirties stands as one of the most relatable stories of womanhood written in the 21st century. Let’s not forget, she also starred in the movie Valentine’s Day in 2010. It’s while filming the romantic comedy where she met future boyfriend Taylor Lautner. Although they eventually split up, fans have since believed her song Back To December was about their breakup. Moving to the present, now Taylor is with the dreamy NFL heartthrob Travis Kelce, it’s not out of the ordinary for strange themed products to follow suit.

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce themed wrapping paper launches in time Valentine’s Day

Travis and Taylor fans have found the cutest wrapping paper for this Valentine’s Day to channel their love for Traylor. Designer Brittany Paige took inspiration from Taylor’s new love story and launched the wrapping paper with a sweet tribute to the music legend.

The pink wrapping paper features a smiling Travis Kelce wearing the Kansas City Chiefs jersey and Tay Tay in a matching outfit that she wore to one of Trav’s games.

The celebrity couple is captured inside a heart with mini hearts all around them.

The designer wrote a note on Instagram referring to Tay Tay’s shout-out to beau Trav at her recent concert.

She writes: “KARMA IS THE GUY ON THE CHIEFS. The way this relationship has a chokehold for me and has set the standard of my dating life.”

The wrapping paper is available on woodsgrove.com for $16.

Inside Taylor Swift’s Valentine’s Day plans

All eyes are currently on Taylor and Travis as fans are dying to know their upcoming Valentine’s Day plans. A source close to the couple said they are trying to spend the day together, but it is extremely difficult for them due to busy schedules.

“They both plan to be with each other on Valentine’s Day, they just don’t know if it will be overseas or in America,” a source told Daily Mail.

“Some pieces have to fall into place and if one of those pieces is Kansas City making the Super Bowl, though it would be extremely tiring, she plans to come back for the Super Bowl for him if he is in the game.”

The plans are tentative as “it all will figure itself out once Kansas City is either in the Super Bowl or not. But they highly anticipate they will be together on Valentine’s Day,” the insider offered.
